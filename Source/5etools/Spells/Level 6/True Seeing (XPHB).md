# True Seeing

*6th-level divination*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S, M (mushroom powder worth 25+ GP, which the spell consumes)  
**Duration:** 1 hour

---

For the duration, the willing creature you touch has [[Truesight|XPHB]] with a range of 120 feet.
