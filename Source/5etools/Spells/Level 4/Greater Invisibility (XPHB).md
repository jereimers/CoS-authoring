# Greater Invisibility

*4th-level illusion*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

---

A creature you touch has the [[Invisible|XPHB]] condition until the spell ends.
