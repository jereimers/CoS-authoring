## Traits

**Swarm.** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny piranha. The swarm can't regain [[Hit Points|XPHB]] or gain [[Temporary Hit Points|XPHB]].

**Water Breathing.** The swarm can breathe only underwater.
