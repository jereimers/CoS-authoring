## Actions

**Multiattack.** The giant makes two attacks, using Frost Axe or Great Bow in any combination.

**Frost Axe.** m +9, reach 10 ft. @h19 (**2d12 + 6**) Slashing damage plus 9 (**2d8**) Cold damage.

**Great Bow.** r +9, range 150/600 ft. @h17 (**2d10 + 6**) Piercing damage plus 7 (**2d6**) Cold damage, and the target's [[Speed|XPHB]] decreases by 10 feet until the end of its next turn.

**War Cry {@recharge 5}.** The giant or one creature of its choice that can see or hear it gains 16 (`2d10 + 5`) [[Temporary Hit Points|XPHB]] and has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on attack rolls until the start of the giant's next turn.
