# Ezmerelda's Rapier +1

## Description
A finely crafted **+1 rapier** wielded by [[Ezmerelda d'Avenir]], the Vistana vampire hunter. This magical blade is one of her primary weapons in combat, allowing her to strike with deadly precision.

## Properties
- **Type:** Martial Melee Weapon
- **Damage:** 1d8 + 5 piercing (including +1 magical bonus and Dexterity modifier)
- **Attack Bonus:** +8 to hit
- **Properties:** Finesse
- **Rarity:** Uncommon
- **Attunement:** No

## Combat Statistics
As wielded by Ezmerelda:
- **Attack:** +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target
- **Hit:** 9 (1d8 + 5) piercing damage
