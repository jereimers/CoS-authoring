# Gloves of Swimming and Climbing

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon* *(requires attunement)*

While wearing these gloves, climbing and swimming don't cost you extra movement, and you gain a +5 bonus to Strength ([[Athletics|XPHB]]) checks made to climb or swim.
