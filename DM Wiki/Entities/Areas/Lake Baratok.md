# What It Is

Lake Baratok is a large body of water located west of [[Town of Vallaki|Vallaki]] in the wilderness of [[Barovia]].

# What the Party Knows

From [[Victor Vallakovich]] in [[Session 13 - The Lost and The Found|Session 13]], the party learned:

## Khazan's Tower

The lake is home to [[Khazan]]'s wizard tower:

- The tower sits at the end of a gravel causeway that extends into the lake
- Khazan, an archmage, built the tower and imbued it with powerful enchantments
- Most notably, the tower features a protective anti-magic field that prevents other spellcasters from using magic within its influence
- This security measure makes the tower incredibly difficult to breach

## Location and Access

- Lake Baratok is located west of [[Town of Vallaki|Vallaki]]
- Victor doesn't know the exact route to reach it
- He recommends hiring [[Szoldar Szoldarovich]], a wolf hunter who knows the wilderness intimately
- Victor was forced by his father to accompany Szoldar on a hunting expedition last year "in an effort to make him more manly"
- Despite hating the experience, Victor came away impressed by Szoldar's wilderness skills and knowledge of the region

## Connection to Current Events

- A platinum ring bearing Khazan's sigil was found at the site where [[Arabelle]] was abducted
- Victor believes investigating the tower might provide clues about why Arabelle was taken
- The party has agreed to explore the tower and bring back any magical artifacts or tomes they find for Victor's research

## From Session 16

The party traveled to Lake Baratok with [[Szoldar Szoldarovich]] and [[Yevgeni Krushkin]] as guides. The journey took them past the [[Hunters' Shack]], where they discovered the [[Butterfly Shrine]], and then along a winding forest path to the lake.

### The Martikov Cabin

Along the lake shore, they passed an abandoned cabin:
- The guides identified it as the old home of [[Davian Martikov]]'s brother, who died of plague about 30 years ago
- Davian now runs the [[Wizard of Wines]] winery east of [[Krezk]]
- The cabin has been left to decay ever since

### Ezmerelda's Wagon

The party discovered a colorful wagon at the lake bearing the name "Ezmerelda d'Avenir" and warnings about traps. Inside, they met **Eggsmerelda**, a chicken who (via [[Varnish Gothorn]]'s *Speak with Animals* spell) explained:
- Her owner is Ezmerelda d'Avenir, a monster hunter
- Ezmerelda has been gone for several days, searching for something in the woods
- Wolves have been trying to get into the wagon at night

### Rictavio's Connection

Investigating a second set of wagon tracks, the party found:
- Older tracks leading south into the woods toward [[Town of Vallaki|Vallaki]]
- Spatterings of bright yellow paint in the underbrush
- The yellow paint matched the distinctive color of [[Rictavio]]'s carnival wagon from the [[Arasek Stockyard]]
- This placed Rictavio's wagon at Lake Baratok near [[Khazan]]'s tower - the same tower whose sigil appeared on the [[DM Wiki/Entities/Items/Signet Ring|ring]] found at [[Arabelle]]'s abduction site
- The party now knows Rictavio is undeniably connected to [[Arabelle]]'s kidnapping, though his exact role and motives remain unclear
