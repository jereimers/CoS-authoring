## Actions

**Multiattack.** The otyugh makes one Bite attack and two Tentacle attacks.

**Bite.** m +6, reach 5 ft. @h12 (**2d8 + 3**) Piercing damage, and the target has the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition. Whenever the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] target finishes a [[Long Rest|XPHB]], it is subjected to the following effect. con DC 15. @actSaveFail The target's [[Hit Points|XPHB]] maximum decreases by 5 (`1d10`) and doesn't return to normal until the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition ends on the target. @actSaveSuccess The [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition ends.

**Tentacle.** m +6, reach 10 ft. @h12 (**2d8 + 3**) Piercing damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, it has the [[Grappled|XPHB]] condition (escape DC 13) from one of two tentacles.

**Tentacle Slam.** con DC 14, each creature [[Grappled|XPHB]] by the otyugh. @actSaveFail 16 (**3d8 + 3**) Bludgeoning damage, and the target has the [[Stunned|XPHB]] condition until the start of the otyugh's next turn. @actSaveSuccess Half damage only.
