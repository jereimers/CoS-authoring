# Charm of Air Bubbles

*Charm*

*This charm can be bestowed by a {@creature chwinga astronaut* using their Magical Gift action.}

This charm allows you to cast the [[Air Bubble|AAG]] spell (see the Astral Adventurer's Guide) as an action. Once used three times, this charm goes away.
