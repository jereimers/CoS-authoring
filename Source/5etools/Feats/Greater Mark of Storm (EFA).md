# Greater Mark of Storm

**Prerequisites:** Level 4

You gain the following benefits.

## Improved Intuition

When you use the Windwright's Intuition benefit of your [[Mark of Storm|EFA]] feat, you can roll `1d6` instead of `1d4`.

## Improved Storm

When you use the Storm Magic benefit of your [[Mark of Storm|EFA]] feat to cast [[Gust of Wind|XPHB]] without a spell slot, you also gain a [[Fly Speed|XPHB]] of 60 feet for the duration of the spell.
