# Hooded Lantern

*Item, None*

A Hooded Lantern burns Oil as fuel to cast [[Bright Light|XPHB]] in a 30-foot radius and [[Dim Light|XPHB]] for an additional 30 feet. As a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]], you can lower the hood, reducing the light to [[Dim Light|XPHB]] in a 5-foot radius, or raise it again.
