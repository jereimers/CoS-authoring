# Crystal Ball of True Seeing

*Wondrous Item, Legendary* *(requires attunement)*

While touching this crystal orb, you can cast [[Scrying|XPHB]] (save DC 17) with it. In addition, you have [[Truesight|XPHB]] with a range of 120 feet centered on the spell's sensor.
