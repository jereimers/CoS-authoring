# Ezmerelda's Potions of Greater Healing

## Description
[[Ezmerelda d'Avenir]] carries **two potions of greater healing** as part of her vampire-hunting equipment. These crimson potions are essential supplies for any monster hunter, providing significant healing in the midst of dangerous encounters.

## Properties
- **Type:** Potion
- **Effect:** Regain 4d4 + 4 hit points when consumed
- **Quantity:** 2
- **Rarity:** Uncommon
- **Attunement:** No

## Usage
Drinking or administering a potion takes an action. When consumed, the drinker regains 4d4 + 4 hit points (average 14 HP).

## Notes
- Ezmerelda carries exactly two of these potions
- An essential part of her monster-hunting supplies
- Can be a valuable resource if Ezmerelda allies with the party
