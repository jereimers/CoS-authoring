# Animal Friendship

*1st-level enchantment*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 30 feet  
**Components:** V, S, M (a morsel of food)  
**Duration:** 24 hours

---

Target a Beast that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the [[Charmed|XPHB]] condition for the duration. If you or one of your allies deals damage to the target, the spells ends.

## Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot

You can target one additional Beast for each spell slot level above 1.
