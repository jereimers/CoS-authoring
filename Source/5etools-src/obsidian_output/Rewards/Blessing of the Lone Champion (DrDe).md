# Blessing of the Lone Champion

*Blessing*

You gain the following benefits:

- **Heroic Inspiration:** You gain [[Heroic Inspiration|XPHB]] when you finish a [[Short Rest|XPHB]] or [[Long Rest|XPHB]] and whenever the DM has you roll Initiative.
- **Temporary Hit Points:** You gain a number of [[Temporary Hit Points|XPHB]] equal to 10 times your level when you gain this blessing and when you gain a level.
