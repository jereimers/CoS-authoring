## Traits

**Incorporeal Movement.** The poltergeist can move through other creatures and objects as if they were [[Difficult Terrain|XPHB]]. It takes 5 (**1d10**) Force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The poltergeist makes one Object Slam attack and uses Telekinetic Thrust.

**Object Slam.** m,r +4, reach 5 ft. or range 30 ft. @h7 (**2d4 + 2**) Bludgeoning damage.

**Telekinetic Thrust.** str DC 12, one creature the poltergeist can see within 30 feet. @actSaveFail 9 (**2d6 + 2**) Force damage, and the target is pushed up to 30 feet straight away from the poltergeist.


## Bonus Actions

**Vanish.** The poltergeist gives itself the [[Invisible|XPHB]] condition or ends that condition on itself.
