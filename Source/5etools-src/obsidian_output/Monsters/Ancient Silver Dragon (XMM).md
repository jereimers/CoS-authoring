## Traits

**Legendary Resistance (4/Day, or 5/Day in Lair).** If the dragon fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The dragon makes three Rend attacks. It can replace one attack with a use of (A) Paralyzing Breath or (B) Spellcasting to cast [[Ice Knife|XPHB]] (level 2 version).

**Rend.** m +17, reach 15 ft. @h19 (**2d8 + 10**) Slashing damage plus 9 (**2d8**) Cold damage.

**Cold Breath {@recharge 5}.** con DC 24, each creature in a 90-foot [[Cone [Area of Effect]|XPHB]]. @actSaveFail 67 (**15d8**) Cold damage. @actSaveSuccess Half damage.

**Paralyzing Breath.** con DC 24, each creature in a 90-foot [[Cone [Area of Effect]|XPHB]]. 1 The target has the [[Incapacitated|XPHB]] condition until the end of its next turn, when it repeats the save. 2 The target has the [[Paralyzed|XPHB]] condition, and it repeats the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. After 1 minute, it succeeds automatically.




## Legendary Actions

The creature can take 3 legendary actions.

**Chill.** The dragon uses Spellcasting to cast [[Hold Monster|XPHB]]. The dragon can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.

**Cold Gale.** dex DC 23, each creature in a 60-foot-long, 10-foot-wide [[Line [Area of Effect]|XPHB]]. @actSaveFail 14 (**4d6**) Cold damage, and the target is pushed up to 30 feet straight away from the dragon. @actSaveSuccess Half damage only. @actSaveSuccessOrFail The dragon can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.

**Pounce.** The dragon moves up to half its [[Speed|XPHB]], and it makes one Rend attack.
