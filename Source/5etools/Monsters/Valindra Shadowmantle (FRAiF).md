## Traits

**Legendary Resistance (4/Day or 5/Day in Lair).** If Valindra fails a saving throw, she can choose to succeed instead.

**Spirit Gem.** If destroyed, Valindra re-forms in `1d10` days if her spirit gem is intact, reviving with all her [[Hit Points|XPHB]]. The new body appears in an unoccupied space within her lair.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** Valindra makes three attacks, using Eldritch Burst or Paralyzing Touch in any combination. She can replace two attacks with a use of Spellcasting to cast [[Lightning Bolt|XPHB]] (level 5 version).

**Eldritch Burst.** m,r +12, reach 5 ft. or range 120 ft. @h31 (**4d12 + 5**) Force damage.

**Paralyzing Touch.** m +12, reach 5 ft. @h14 (**2d8 + 5**) Cold damage, and the target has the [[Paralyzed|XPHB]] condition until the start of Valindra's next turn.

**Deathly Grasp {@recharge 5}.** dex DC 20, each creature in a 30-foot [[Emanation [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] originating from Valindra. 1 39 (**6d12**) Force damage and the target has the [[Restrained|XPHB]] condition and repeats the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. After 1 minute, it succeeds automatically. Subsequent Failures: 39 (**6d12**) Necrotic damage, and Valindra gains 20 [[Temporary Hit Points|XPHB]].




## Legendary Actions

The creature can take 3 legendary actions.

**Disrupt Life.** con DC 20, each creature that isn't Undead in a 20-foot [[Emanation [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] originating from Valindra. @actSaveFail 21 (**6d6**) Necrotic damage. @actSaveSuccess Half damage. @actSaveSuccessOrFail Valindra can't take this action again until the start of her next turn.

**Eldritch Jaunt.** Valindra makes one Eldritch Burst attack. Before or after the attack, Valindra can teleport up to 20 feet to an unoccupied space she can see.

**Life-Rending Gaze.** wis DC 20, one creature Valindra can see within 30 feet. @actSaveFail 22 (**4d10**) Necrotic damage, and the target has the [[Frightened|XPHB]] condition until the end of its next turn. @actSaveSuccess Half damage only.
