# Niko's Mace

*Item, Very Rare* *(requires attunement by a spellcaster)*

This Mace has 6 charges and regains `1d6` expended charges daily at dawn. While holding the Mace, you can expend 1 of its charges to cast [[Summon Celestial|XPHB]] (+9 to hit with spell attacks).
