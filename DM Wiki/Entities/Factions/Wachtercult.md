# Overview

Unlike in the official *Curse of Strahd* module, [[Lady Fiona Wachter]] does not worship devils. Instead, although she retains an imp familiar as a memento of her time studying with the witch Baba Lysaga, she has taught the members of her Vallakian cult to worship the goddess [[Ezra, God of the Mists|Ezra]].

# Organization

Currently, Lady Wachter's "book club" boasts six cult fanatics, with each fanatic leading a smaller cell of six cultists. The cult operates secretly within [[Town of Vallaki|Vallaki]], working to undermine [[Baron Vargas Vallakovich|Baron Vallakovich's]] rule and prepare for Lady Wachter's ascension to power.
