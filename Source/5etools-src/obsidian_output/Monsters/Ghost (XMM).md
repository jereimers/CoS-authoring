## Traits

**Ethereal Sight.** The ghost can see 60 feet into the Ethereal Plane when it is on the Material Plane.

**Incorporeal Movement.** The ghost can move through other creatures and objects as if they were [[Difficult Terrain|XPHB]]. It takes 5 (**1d10**) Force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.
