## Traits

**Legendary Resistance (3/Day).** If the unicorn fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

**Magic Resistance.** The unicorn has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The unicorn makes one Hooves attack and one Radiant Horn attack.

**Hooves.** m +7, reach 5 ft. @h11 (**2d6 + 4**) Bludgeoning damage.

**Radiant Horn.** m +7, reach 5 ft. @h9 (**1d10 + 4**) Radiant damage.




## Legendary Actions

The creature can take 3 legendary actions.

**Charging Horn.** The unicorn moves up to half its [[Speed|XPHB]] without provoking [[Opportunity Attack|XPHB]], and it makes one Radiant Horn attack.

**Shimmering Shield.** The unicorn targets itself or one creature it can see within 60 feet of itself. The target gains 10 (`3d6`) [[Temporary Hit Points|XPHB]], and its AC increases by 2 until the end of the unicorn's next turn. The unicorn can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.
