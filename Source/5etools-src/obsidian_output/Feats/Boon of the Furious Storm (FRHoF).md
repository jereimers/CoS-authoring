# Boon of the Furious Storm

**Prerequisites:** Level 19

You gain the following benefits.

## Eye of the Storm

You have [[Resistance|XPHB]] to Lightning and Thunder damage. While you are [[Bloodied]], you have [[Immunity|XPHB]] to Lightning and Thunder damage.

## Storm's Strength

Creatures have [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against your spells that deal Lightning or Thunder damage.
