# Overview

Three of [[Source/CoS-Reloaded/Chapter 2 - The Land of Barovia/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd's]] eldest vampiric consorts—Ludmilla, Anastrasya, and [[Volenta Popofsky|Volenta]]—serve their master loyally, but compete bitterly for his favor.

# The Three Brides

## Ludmilla Vilisevic

As the eldest and most adept at the arcane arts, Ludmilla is Strahd's favored research partner, developing new spells for his use while investigating the secrets of the Mists.

## Anastrasya Karelova

Anastrasya is a master of the arts of espionage and diplomacy, managing Strahd's political relationships while retaining key spies in each of [[Barovia]]'s settlements.

## Volenta Popofsky

[[Volenta Popofsky|Volenta]] is a skilled artificer and torturer, innovating new traps to guard Ravenloft's halls while wringing information from those who fall into them.

# Their Awakening

Over the past hundred years, with Strahd asleep in [[Castle Ravenloft]], each of his brides dispersed through the valley to pursue their own agendas. Now, with Strahd awoken, and his moment of reckoning at hand, each bride is desperate to prove their loyalty and worth to Strahd once more—and to win their place at his side when his grand plan comes to fruition.
