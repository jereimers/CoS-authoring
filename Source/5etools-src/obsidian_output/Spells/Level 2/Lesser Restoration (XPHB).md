# Lesser Restoration

*2nd-level abjuration*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 bonus  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

You touch a creature and end one condition on it: [[Blinded|XPHB]], [[Deafened|XPHB]], [[Paralyzed|XPHB]], or [[Poisoned|XPHB]].
