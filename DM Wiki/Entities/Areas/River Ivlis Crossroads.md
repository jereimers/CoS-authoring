# Overview

The River Ivlis Crossroads is a junction along the [[Old Svalich Road]] where multiple paths converge near a small cemetery. It marks a significant waypoint between the [[Village of Barovia]] and the [[Tser Pool Encampment]], and has become a site of mystical significance—[[Madam Eva]] chose this location for the party's Tarokka reading, and it is here that the [[03 - Village of Barovia - March of the Dead|March of the Dead]] can be witnessed each midnight.

# What the party knows

- This crossroads served as the meeting place for [[Madam Eva]]'s Tarokka reading
- At midnight, the [[03 - Village of Barovia - March of the Dead|March of the Dead]] can be witnessed here—the ghostly procession of failed heroes marching toward [[Castle Ravenloft]]
- The party's first encounter with [[Strahd von Zarovich|the Devil Strahd]] occurred near this location
- They have passed through the crossroads multiple times on their journey

# Notable places

- The crossroads cemetery
- The junction of roads leading to [[Village of Barovia]], [[Tser Pool Encampment]], and [[Town of Vallaki|Vallaki]]

# Notable figures

- [[Madam Eva]] (met here for the Tarokka reading)
