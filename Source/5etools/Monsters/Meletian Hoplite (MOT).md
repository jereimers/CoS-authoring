**Multiattack.** The hoplite makes three weapon attacks. It can replace one weapon attack with ray of frost.

**Spear.** Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h6 (**1d6 + 3**) piercing damage, or 7 (**1d8 + 3**) piercing damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack.

**Shield Bash.** Melee Weapon Attack +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. @h5 (**1d4 + 3**) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, it must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked [[prone]].

**Ray of Frost (Cantrip).** Ranged Spell Attack +5 to hit, range 60 ft., one creature. @h4 (**1d8**) cold damage, and the target's speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of the hoplite's next turn.
