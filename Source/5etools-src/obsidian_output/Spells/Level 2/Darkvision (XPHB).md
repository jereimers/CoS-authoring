# Darkvision

*2nd-level transmutation*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S, M (a dried carrot)  
**Duration:** 8 hours

---

For the duration, a willing creature you touch has [[Darkvision|XPHB]] with a range of 150 feet.
