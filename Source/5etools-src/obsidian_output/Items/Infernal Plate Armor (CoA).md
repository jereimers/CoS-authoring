# Infernal Plate Armor

*Item, Very Rare* *(requires attunement)*

While wearing this armor, you gain a +2 bonus to AC.

## Curse

Once you wear this armor, and are attuned to it, you can't remove it. Only the [[Remove Curse]] spell allows you to end the attunement and finally doff it. While wearing the armor, you're vulnerable to the following damage types: force, lightning, psychic, radiant, and thunder.

## Corrupting

This item corrupts. See the "[[Infernal Item Corruption|CoA]]" section.
