# Protection from Poison

*2nd-level abjuration*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** 1 hour

---

You touch a creature and end the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition on it. For the duration, the target has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws to avoid or end the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition, and it has [[Resistance|XPHB]] to Poison damage.
