# Kasimir's Ring of Warmth

## Description
A **ring of warmth** worn by [[Kasimir Velikov]], the dusk elf mage who lives among the Vistani near Vallaki. This magical ring protects the wearer from cold temperatures and cold damage—essential protection for anyone living in the often harsh climate of Barovia.

For Kasimir, who spends much of his time studying and preparing for a journey to the Amber Temple high in the frozen mountains, this ring is a practical necessity.

## Properties
- **Type:** Ring
- **Rarity:** Uncommon
- **Attunement:** Yes

## Magic
While wearing this ring, you have resistance to cold damage. In addition, you and everything you wear and carry are unharmed by temperatures as low as −50 degrees Fahrenheit (−46 degrees Celsius).
