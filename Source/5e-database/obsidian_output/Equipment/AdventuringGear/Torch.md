# Torch

*Adventuring Gear*

**Cost:** 1 cp
**Weight:** 1 lb.

A torch burns for 1 hour, providing bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet. If you make a melee attack with a burning torch and hit, it deals 1 fire damage.
