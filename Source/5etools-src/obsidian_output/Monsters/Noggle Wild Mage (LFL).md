## Actions

**Multiattack.** The noggle makes two attacks, using Noggling Stick or Befuddling Ray in any combination.

**Noggling Stick.** m +5, reach 5 ft. @h5 (**1d4 + 3**) Bludgeoning damage plus 2 (**1d4**) Psychic damage.

**Befuddling Ray.** r +5, range 60 ft. @h6 (**1d6 + 3**) Psychic damage, and the target subtracts `1d4` from the next saving throw it makes before the end of the noggle's next turn.
