**Vampire Weakness.** The vampire has these weaknesses:

- The vampire can't enter a residence without an invitation from an occupant.
- The vampire takes 20 Acid damage if it ends its turn in running water.
- If a weapon that deals Piercing damage is driven into the vampire's heart while the vampire has the [[Incapacitated|XPHB]] condition, the vampire has the [[Paralyzed|XPHB]] condition until the weapon is removed.
- The vampire takes 20 Radiant damage if it starts its turn in sunlight. While in sunlight, it has [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on attack rolls and ability checks.
