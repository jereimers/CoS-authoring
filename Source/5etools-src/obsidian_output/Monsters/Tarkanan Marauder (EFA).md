## Traits

**Evasion.** If the marauder is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, the marauder instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the save and only half as much damage if it fails. It can't use this trait if it has the [[Incapacitated|XPHB]] condition.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The marauder makes three Aberrant Strike attacks.

**Aberrant Strike.** m +7, reach 5 ft. @h13 (**3d6 + 3**) Piercing damage, plus 21 (**6d6**) Force damage if the marauder had [[Advantage|XPHB]] on the attack roll.


## Bonus Actions

**Tear Through Space.** The marauder teleports to a space it can see within 30 feet, appearing in a dazzling flash. con DC 17, each creature in a 5-foot [[Emanation [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] originating from the marauder when it appears. @actSaveFail The target has the [[Blinded|XPHB]] condition until the end of the marauder's next turn.
