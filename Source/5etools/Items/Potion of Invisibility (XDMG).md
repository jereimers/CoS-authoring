# Potion of Invisibility

*Item, Rare*

This potion's container looks empty but feels as though it holds liquid. When you drink the potion, you have the [[Invisible|XPHB]] condition for 1 hour. The effect ends early if you make an attack roll, deal damage, or cast a spell.
