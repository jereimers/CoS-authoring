## Traits

**Pack Tactics.** The crawler has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the crawler's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't [[incapacitated]].


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The crawler makes four attacks: one with its bite, two with its claws, and one with its tail.

**Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. @h4 (**1d4 + 2**) piercing damage and the target must succeed on a DC 11 Wisdom saving throw or become [[frightened]] until the start of the crawler's next turn.

**Claws.** Melee Weapon Attack +4 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. @h4 (**1d4 + 2**) slashing damage.

**Tail.** Melee Weapon Attack +4 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. @h5 (**1d6 + 2**) piercing damage.
