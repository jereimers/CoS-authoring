# Healer's Kit

*Item, None*

A Healer's Kit has ten uses. As a [[Utilize|XPHB]] action, you can expend one of its uses to stabilize an [[Unconscious|XPHB]] creature that has 0 [[Hit Points|XPHB]] without needing to make a Wisdom ([[Medicine|XPHB]]) check.
