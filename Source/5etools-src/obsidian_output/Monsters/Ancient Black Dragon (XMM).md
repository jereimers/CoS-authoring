The creature can take 3 legendary actions.

**Cloud of Insects.** dex DC 21, one creature the dragon can see within 120 feet. @actSaveFail 33 (**6d10**) Poison damage, and the target has [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on saving throws to maintain [[Concentration]] until the end of its next turn. @actSaveSuccessOrFail The dragon can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.

**Pounce.** The dragon moves up to half its [[Speed|XPHB]], and it makes one Rend attack.
