# Rahadin's Poisoned Darts

## Description
[[Rahadin]], the dusk elf chamberlain of [[Castle Ravenloft]], carries a supply of **poisoned darts** as a ranged alternative to his scimitar. These darts are coated with a deadly poison, allowing Rahadin to eliminate targets from a distance with lethal efficiency.

## Properties
- **Type:** Simple Ranged Weapon (Dart) + Poison
- **Damage:** 1d4 + 6 piercing plus 2d4 poison
- **Attack Bonus:** +10 to hit
- **Range:** 20/60 ft.
- **Properties:** Finesse, Thrown, Poisoned
- **Rarity:** Common (mundane with applied poison)
- **Attunement:** No

## Combat Statistics
As wielded by Rahadin:
- **Attack:** +10 to hit, range 20/60 ft., one target
- **Hit:** 8 (1d4 + 6) piercing damage plus 5 (2d4) poison damage
- **Total Average Damage:** 13 per dart
- **Multiattack:** Rahadin can attack twice with his poisoned darts
