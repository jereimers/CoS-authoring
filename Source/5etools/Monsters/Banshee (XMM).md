**Detect Life.** The banshee magically senses the direction of creatures up to 1 mile away that aren't Constructs or Undead.

**Incorporeal Movement.** The banshee can move through other creatures and objects as if they were [[Difficult Terrain|XPHB]]. It takes 5 (**1d10**) Force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.
