# Heal

*6th-level abjuration*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 60 feet  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

Choose a creature that you can see within range. Positive energy washes through the target, restoring 70 [[Hit Points|XPHB]]. This spell also ends the [[Blinded|XPHB]], [[Deafened|XPHB]], and [[Poisoned|XPHB]] conditions on the target.

## Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot

The healing increases by `70` for each spell slot level above 6.
