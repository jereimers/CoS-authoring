# Ram, portable

*Adventuring Gear*

**Cost:** 4 gp
**Weight:** 35 lb.

You can use a portable ram to break down doors. When doing so, you gain a +4 bonus on the Strength check. One other character can help you use the ram, giving you advantage on this check.
