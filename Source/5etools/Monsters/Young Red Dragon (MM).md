## Actions

**Multiattack.** The dragon makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws.

**Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. @h17 (**2d10 + 6**) piercing damage plus 3 (**1d6**) fire damage.

**Claw.** Melee Weapon Attack +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h13 (**2d6 + 6**) slashing damage.

**Fire Breath {@recharge 5}.** The dragon exhales fire in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 56 (**16d6**) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
