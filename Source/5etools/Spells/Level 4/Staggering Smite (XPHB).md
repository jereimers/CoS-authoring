# Staggering Smite

*4th-level enchantment*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 bonus (which you take immediately after hitting a creature with a Melee weapon or an {@variantrule Unarmed Strike|XPHB})  
**Range:** Self  
**Components:** V  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

The target takes an extra **4d6** Psychic damage from the attack, and the target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the [[Stunned|XPHB]] condition until the end of your next turn.

## Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot

The extra damage increases by `4d6` for each spell slot level above 4.
