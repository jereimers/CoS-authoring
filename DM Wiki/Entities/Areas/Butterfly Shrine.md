# What It Is

An ancient shrine consisting of seven standing stones arranged in a rough circle near the [[Hunters' Shack]] on the northern shore of [[Lake Zarovich]]. One of the stones bears a carved butterfly symbol. The origin and purpose of the shrine remain unknown.

# What the Party Knows

## From Session 16

The party discovered the Butterfly Shrine in [[Session 16 - The Lady, the Witch, and the Ringmaster|Session 16]]:

- Located near the [[Hunters' Shack]] on the northern shore of [[Lake Zarovich]]
- Features seven standing stones arranged in a rough circle
- Each stone is taller than a man and covered in moss and age
- One stone is carved with a butterfly symbol
- The stones radiate the same quiet, patient power felt at the [[Raven Shrine]]
- [[Szoldar Szoldarovich]] and [[Yevgeni Krushkin]] leave them alone, not knowing their origin or purpose but unwilling to anger whatever entities inspired their construction
- The party left an offering of gold coins at the base of the butterfly-carved stone
- The similarity to the [[Raven Shrine]] near the [[Village of Barovia]] suggests these shrines may be part of a larger network of ancient sacred sites
