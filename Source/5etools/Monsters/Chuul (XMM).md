## Traits

**Amphibious.** The chuul can breathe air and water.

**Sense Magic.** The chuul senses magic within 120 feet of itself. This trait otherwise works like the [[Detect Magic|XPHB]] spell but isn't itself magical.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The chuul makes two Pincer attacks and uses Paralyzing Tentacles.

**Pincer.** m +6, reach 10 ft. @h9 (**1d10 + 4**) Bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it has the [[Grappled|XPHB]] condition (escape DC 14) from one of two pincers.

**Paralyzing Tentacles.** con DC 13, one creature [[Grappled|XPHB]] by the chuul. @actSaveFail The target has the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition and repeats the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. After 1 minute, it succeeds automatically. While [[Poisoned|XPHB]], the target has the [[Paralyzed|XPHB]] condition.
