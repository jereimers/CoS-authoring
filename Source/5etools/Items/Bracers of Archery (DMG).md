# Bracers of Archery

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon* *(requires attunement)*

While wearing these bracers, you have proficiency with the [[longbow|PHB]] and [[shortbow|PHB]], and you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls on ranged attacks made with such weapons.
