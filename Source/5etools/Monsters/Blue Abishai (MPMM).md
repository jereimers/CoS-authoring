# Blue Abishai

*Medium fiend (devil, wizard), Lawful Evil*

---

**Armor Class** 19 (natural armor)  
**Hit Points** 202 (27d8 + 81)  
**Speed** walk 30 ft., fly 50 ft.

---

| **STR** 15 (+2) | **DEX** 14 (+2) | **CON** 17 (+3) | **INT** 22 (+6) | **WIS** 23 (+6) | **CHA** 18 (+4) |

---

**Saving Throws** INT +12, WIS +12  
**Skills** Arcana +12  
**Damage Resistances** cold  
**Damage Immunities** fire, lightning, poison  
**Condition Immunities** poisoned  
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 16  
**Languages** Draconic, Infernal, telepathy 120 ft.  
**Challenge** 17 (18,000 XP)

---

## Traits

**Devil's Sight.** Magical darkness doesn't impede the abishai's [[darkvision]].

**Magic Resistance.** The abishai has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The abishai makes three Bite or Lightning Strike attacks.

**Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h13 (**2d10 + 2**) piercing damage plus 14 (**4d6**) lightning damage.

**Lightning Strike.** Ranged Spell Attack +12 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. @h36 (**8d8**) lightning damage.


## Bonus Actions

**Teleport.** The abishai teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space that it can see.
