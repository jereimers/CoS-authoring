# Boon of the Night Spirit

**Prerequisites:** Level 19

You gain the following benefits.

## Merge with Shadows

While within [[Dim Light|XPHB]] or [[Darkness|XPHB]], you can give yourself the [[Invisible|XPHB]] condition as a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]]. The condition ends on you immediately after you take an action, a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]], or a [[Reaction|XPHB]].

## Shadowy Form

While within [[Dim Light|XPHB]] or [[Darkness|XPHB]], you have [[Resistance|XPHB]] to all damage except Psychic and Radiant.
