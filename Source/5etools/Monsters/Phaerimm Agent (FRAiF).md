## Traits

**Magic Resistance.** The phaerimm has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The phaerimm makes three attacks, using Dread Stinger or Mindwarp Ray in any combination.

**Dread Stinger.** m +7, reach 5 ft. @h7 (**1d6 + 4**) Piercing damage plus 13 (**2d12**) Poison damage.

**Mindwarp Ray.** r +7, range 120 ft. @h13 (**2d8 + 4**) Psychic damage, and the target has the [[Charmed|XPHB]] condition until the start of the phaerimm's next turn.


## Bonus Actions

**Teleport.** The phaerimm teleports up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.
