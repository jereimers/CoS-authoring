# Overview

The people of Vallaki live under the rule of [[Baron Vargas Vallakovich]], the local burgomaster. Though few townsfolk view him with much love or respect, the Baron commands the loyalty of [[Izek Strazni]], a cruel and capricious brute who enforces the Baron's will with an iron fist.

# Rule by Fear

[[Izek Strazni]], as Captain of the Guard, commands the loyalty—and, more importantly, the salaries—of the town's twenty-four guards. Though Vallaki is far from a police state, Izek has spies throughout the town—individuals that he has threatened or intimidated into keeping him abreast of news and information.

Those who speak ill of the Baron or his "festivals" are thrown in the stocks. Those who go further may find themselves forcibly summoned to the Baron's manor for "corrective rehabilitation," or even left to the tender mercies of Izek himself, whose diabolic mastery of fire allows him to burn his victims' flesh or set their homes aflame.

# The People's Response

As a result, the vast majority of Vallakians have surrendered themselves to [[Baron Vargas Vallakovich|Baron Vallakovich's]] rule, too terrified of Izek to dare speak out. The sole exceptions are [[Lady Fiona Wachter]] and her followers, who worship [[Ezra, God of the Mists|Ezra]] and seek to end the Baron's oppressive reign.
