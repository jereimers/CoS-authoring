# Restrained

While you have the Restrained condition, you experience the following effects.

### Speed 0

Your [[Speed|XPHB]] is 0 and can't increase.

### Attacks Affected

[[Attack|XPHB]] rolls against you have [[Advantage|XPHB]], and your attack rolls have [[Disadvantage|XPHB]].

### Saving Throws Affected

You have [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on Dexterity [[Saving Throw|XPHB]].
