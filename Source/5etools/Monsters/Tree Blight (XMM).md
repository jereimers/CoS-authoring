## Actions

**Multiattack.** The blight makes two Branch attacks and uses Grasping Root.

**Branch.** m +9, reach 15 ft. @h16 (**3d6 + 6**) Bludgeoning damage.

**Grasping Root.** str DC 17, one Large or smaller creature the blight can see within 15 feet. @actSaveFail The target is pulled up to 10 feet straight toward the blight and has the [[Grappled|XPHB]] condition (escape DC 16) from one of six roots. Until the grapple ends, the target takes 13 (**2d6 + 6**) Bludgeoning damage at the start of each of its turns.


## Bonus Actions

**Gnash.** dex DC 17, one creature [[Grappled|XPHB]] by the blight. @actSaveFail 19 (**3d8 + 6**) Piercing damage. @actSaveSuccess Half damage.
