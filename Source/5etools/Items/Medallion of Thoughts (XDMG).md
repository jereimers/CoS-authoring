# Medallion of Thoughts

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon* *(requires attunement)*

The medallion has 5 charges. While wearing it, you can expend 1 charge to cast [[Detect Thoughts|XPHB]] (save DC 13) from it. The medallion regains `1d4` expended charges daily at dawn.
