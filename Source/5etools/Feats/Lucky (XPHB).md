# Lucky

You gain the following benefits.

## Luck Points

You have a number of Luck Points equal to your [[Proficiency|XPHB]] and can spend the points on the benefits below. You regain your expended Luck Points when you finish a [[Long Rest|XPHB]].

## Advantage

When you roll a `d20` for a [[D20 Test|XPHB]], you can spend 1 Luck Point to give yourself [[Advantage|XPHB]] on the roll.

## Disadvantage

When a creature rolls a `d20` for an attack roll against you, you can spend 1 Luck Point to impose [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on that roll.
