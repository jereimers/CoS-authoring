## Traits

**Shapechanger.** The yuan-ti can use its action to polymorph into a Medium snake, or back into its true form. Its statistics are the same in each form. Any equipment it is wearing or carrying isn't transformed. It doesn't change form if it dies.

**Magic Resistance.** The yuan-ti has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**Malison Type.** The yuan-ti has one of the following types:

- **Type 4::** Human form with one or more serpentine tails
- **Type 5::** Human form covered in scales


## Actions

**Multiattack (Yuan-ti Form Only).** The yuan-ti makes two ranged attacks or two melee attacks.

**Bite (Snake Form Only).** Melee Weapon Attack +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. @h5 (**1d4 + 3**) piercing damage plus 7 (**2d6**) poison damage.

**Scimitar (Yuan-ti Form Only).** Melee Weapon Attack +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h6 (**1d6 + 3**) slashing damage.

**Longbow (Yuan-ti Form Only).** Ranged Weapon Attack +4 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. @h6 (**1d8 + 2**) piercing damage.
