# Dwarf

**Size:** Medium  
**Speed:** 30  

## Darkvision

You have [[Darkvision|XPHB]] with a range of 120 feet.

## Dwarven Resilience

You have [[Resistance|XPHB]] to Poison damage. You also have [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws you make to avoid or end the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition.

## Dwarven Toughness

Your [[Hit Points|XPHB]] maximum increases by 1, and it increases by 1 again whenever you gain a level.

## Stonecunning

As a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]], you gain [[Tremorsense|XPHB]] with a range of 60 feet for 10 minutes. You must be on a stone surface or touching a stone surface to use this [[Tremorsense|XPHB]]. The stone can be natural or worked.

You can use this [[Bonus Action|XPHB]] a number of times equal to your [[Proficiency|XPHB]], and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [[Long Rest|XPHB]].
