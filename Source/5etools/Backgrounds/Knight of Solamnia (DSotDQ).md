# Knight of Solamnia

- **Skill Proficiencies::** [[Athletics]], [[Survival]]
- **Languages::** Two of your choice
- **Equipment::** An insignia of rank, a [[playing card set|phb]], a set of [[common clothes|PHB]], and a [[pouch|PHB]] containing 10 gp

## Feature: Squire of Solamnia

You gain the [[Squire of Solamnia|DSotDQ]] feat.

In addition, the Knights of Solamnia provide you free, modest lodging and food at any of their fortresses or encampments.

## Building a Knight of Solamnia Character

Any class that has martial prowess can be a good fit in the Knights of Solamnia. [[Fighter|]] and [[paladin|]] make up the bulk of the knighthood's forces. [[Cleric|]] (often with the [[Cleric|PHB]] domain) can also be found among the knights' ranks.

For a more unusual take on a Knight of Solamnia character, consider playing a bard of the [[Bard|PHB]] (or the [[Bard|PHB]] from [[Xanathar's Guide to Everything|XGE]]) or a [[barbarian]] devoted to the ideals of the nature god [[Habbakuk|dragonlance]] (perhaps adopting the [[barbarian|]] from [[Xanathar's Guide to Everything|XGE]]).
