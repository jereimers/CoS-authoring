# Jump

*1st-level transmutation*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S, M (A grasshopper's hind leg.)  
**Duration:** 1 minute

---

You touch a creature. The creature's jump distance is tripled until the spell ends.
