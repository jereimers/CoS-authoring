## Traits

**Magic Resistance.** The incarnation has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The incarnation makes two Vine attacks and uses Radiant Blast. It can replace one vine attack with a use of Spellcasting to cast [[Blindness/Deafness|XPHB]] if available.

**Vine.** m +9, reach 20 ft. @h18 (**3d8 + 5**) Bludgeoning damage. If the target is the incarnation's size or smaller, it has the [[Grappled|XPHB]] condition (escape DC 15) from one of two vines.

**Radiant Blast.** con DC 17, each creature of the incarnation's choice in a 20-foot [[Emanation [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] originating from the incarnation. @actSaveFail The creature takes 14 (**2d8 + 5**) Radiant damage. @actSaveSuccess Half damage.


## Bonus Actions

**Shape-Shift.** The incarnation changes its size to Medium, Large, or Huge.

**Teleport.** The incarnation teleports up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see. The incarnation can't teleport while it has a creature [[Grappled|XPHB]].
