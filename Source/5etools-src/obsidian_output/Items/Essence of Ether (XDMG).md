# Essence of Ether

*Item, None*

A creature subjected to Essence of Ether must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or have the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition for 8 hours. The creature also has the [[Unconscious|XPHB]] condition while [[Poisoned|XPHB]] in this way. The creature wakes up if it takes damage or if another creature takes an action to shake it awake.
