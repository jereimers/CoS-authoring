## Traits

**Magic Resistance.** The armanite has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The armanite makes one Claw attack, one Hooves attack, and one Serrated Tail attack.

**Claw.** Melee Weapon Attack +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h10 (**2d4 + 5**) slashing damage plus 9 (**2d8**) lightning damage.

**Hooves.** Melee Weapon Attack +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h12 (**2d6 + 5**) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be knocked [[prone]].

**Serrated Tail.** Melee Weapon Attack +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. @h16 (**2d10 + 5**) slashing damage.

**Lightning Lance {@recharge 5}.** The armanite looses a bolt of lightning in a line that is 60 feet long and 10 feet wide. Each creature in the line must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 36 (**8d8**) lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
