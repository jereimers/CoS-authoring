# Deathdance

*Item, None*

A creature subjected to Deathdance must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition for 1 minute. Humanoids automatically succeed on this save. The creature also has the [[Incapacitated|XPHB]] condition while [[Poisoned|XPHB]] in this way, as it thrashes uncontrollably.
