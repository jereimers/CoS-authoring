# Sentinel

**Prerequisites:** Level 4, STR 13+, Level 4, DEX 13+

You gain the following benefits.

## Guardian

Immediately after a creature within 5 feet of you takes the [[Disengage|XPHB]] action or hits a target other than you with an attack, you can make an [[Opportunity Attack|XPHB]] against that creature.

## Halt

When you hit a creature with an [[Opportunity Attack|XPHB]], the creature's [[Speed|XPHB]] becomes 0 for the rest of the current turn.
