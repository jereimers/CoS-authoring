# Boon of Looming Shadows

**Prerequisites:** Level 19

You gain the following benefits.

## Shadowy Stretch

When you take the [[Attack|XPHB]] action, your reach for your Melee weapon attacks increases by 10 feet until the end of your turn.

## Dancing Silhouette

You can take the [[Dodge|XPHB]] action as a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]].
