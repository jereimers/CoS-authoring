# Chill Touch

*Cantrip necromancy*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

Channeling the chill of the grave, make a melee spell attack against a target within reach. On a hit, the target takes **1d10** Necrotic damage, and it can't regain [[Hit Points|XPHB]] until the end of your next turn.

## Cantrip Upgrade

The damage increases by **1d10** when you reach levels 5 (**2d10**), 11 (**3d10**), and 17 (**4d10**).
