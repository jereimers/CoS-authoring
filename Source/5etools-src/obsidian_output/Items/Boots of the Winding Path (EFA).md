# Boots of the Winding Path

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon* *(requires attunement)*

While wearing these boots, you can take a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]] to teleport up to 15 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. You must have occupied that space at some point during the current turn.
