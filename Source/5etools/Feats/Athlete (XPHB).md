# Athlete

**Prerequisites:** Level 4, STR 13+, Level 4, DEX 13+

You gain the following benefits.

## Climb Speed

You gain a [[Climb Speed|XPHB]] equal to your [[Speed|XPHB]].

## Hop Up

When you have the [[Prone|XPHB]] condition, you can right yourself with only 5 feet of movement.

## Jumping

You can make a running Long or [[High Jump|XPHB]] after moving only 5 feet.
