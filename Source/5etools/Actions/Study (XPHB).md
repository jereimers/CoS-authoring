# Study

**Time:** 1 action

When you take the Study action, you make an Intelligence check to study your memory, a book, a clue, or another source of knowledge and call to mind an important piece of information about it.

The Areas of Knowledge table suggests which skills are applicable to various areas of knowledge.

**Areas of Knowledge**

| Skill | Areas |
|---|---|
| [[Arcana|XPHB]] | Spells, magic items, eldritch symbols, magical traditions, planes of existence, and certain creatures (Aberrations, Constructs, Elementals, Fey, and Monstrosities) |
| [[History|XPHB]] | Historic events and people, ancient civilizations, wars, and certain creatures (Giants and Humanoids) |
| [[Investigation|XPHB]] | Traps, ciphers, riddles, and gadgetry |
| [[Nature|XPHB]] | Terrain, flora, weather, and certain creatures (Beasts, Dragons, Oozes, and Plants) |
| [[Religion|XPHB]] | Deities, religious hierarchies and rites, holy symbols, cults, and certain creatures (Celestials, Fiends, and Undead) |
