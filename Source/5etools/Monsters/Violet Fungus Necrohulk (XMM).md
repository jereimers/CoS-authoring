## Actions

**Rotting Slam.** m +7, reach 10 ft. @h9 (**1d10 + 4**) Bludgeoning damage plus 7 (**2d6**) Necrotic damage.

**Spore Bomb {@recharge 5}.** con DC 15, each creature in a 20-foot-radius [[Sphere [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] centered on a point the necrohulk can see within 60 feet. @actSaveFail 28 (**8d6**) Necrotic damage, and the target has the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition until the start of the necrohulk's next turn. While [[Poisoned|XPHB]], the target can't regain [[Hit Points|XPHB]]. @actSaveSuccess Half damage only.


## Bonus Actions

**Absorb Body.** str DC 15, one Medium or Small creature the necrohulk can see within 5 feet. @actSaveFail The target is pulled into the necrohulk's space and becomes grafted to its body. The necrohulk can have only one target grafted at a time.

While grafted, the target has the [[Restrained|XPHB]] condition and [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on Constitution saving throws. When the necrohulk moves, the grafted target moves with it. If the target dies while grafted, its body is destroyed, and the necrohulk regains 10 [[Hit Points|XPHB]].

The grafted target or a creature within 5 feet of the necrohulk can take an action to make a DC 15 Strength ([[Athletics|XPHB]]) check. On a successful check, the target is no longer grafted and moves to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the necrohulk.
