# Sled Dog

*Item, None*

A sled dog uses the [[Wolf|XMM]] stat block (see the Monster Manual). A sled dog pulling a sled alone must take a [[Short Rest|XPHB]] after pulling a sled for 1 hour; otherwise, it gains 1 [[Exhaustion|XPHB]] level.
