## Actions

**Multiattack.** The veteran makes two longsword attacks. If it has a shortsword drawn, it can also make a shortsword attack.

**Longsword.** Melee Weapon Attack +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h7 (**1d8 + 3**) slashing damage, or 8 (**1d10 + 3**) slashing damage if used with two hands.

**Shortsword.** Melee Weapon Attack +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h6 (**1d6 + 3**) piercing damage.

**Heavy Crossbow.** Ranged Weapon Attack +3 to hit, range 100/400 ft., one target. @h6 (**1d10 + 1**) piercing damage.

**Fire Breath {@recharge 5}.** The veteran exhales fire in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 24 (**7d6**) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
