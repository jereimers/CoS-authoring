# Skull Card

*Wondrous Item, Legendary*

As an action, you can brandish this card and use it to cast [[Spirit of Death|BMT]] (see [[chapter 7|BMT]]) as a 6th-level spell (spell attack bonus +9, save DC 17). Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.
