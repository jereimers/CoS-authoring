# Devil Mask

*Item, None*

While you are wearing a Devil Mask, other creatures have [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on Intelligence ([[Investigation|XPHB]]) and Wisdom ([[Insight|XPHB]]) checks made to discern your true identity or intentions.
