## Traits

**Immutable Form.** The statue is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The statue makes two attacks: one with its blade and one with its hammer.

**Blade.** Melee Weapon Attack +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h10 (**2d6 + 3**) slashing damage.

**Hammer.** Melee Weapon Attack +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h10 (**2d6 + 3**) bludgeoning damage, and the target is knocked [[prone]].

**Whirl {@recharge 5}.** The statue can use its action to spin at the waist, targeting creatures of its choice within 10 feet of it. Each target must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 19 (**3d10 + 3**) bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
