## Actions

**Multiattack.** The necromancer makes three Arcane Burst attacks.

**Arcane Burst.** Attack (ms,rs) +7 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 120 ft., one target. @h25 (**4d10 + 3**) necrotic damage.


## Bonus Actions

**Summon Undead (1/Day).** The necromancer magically summons five [[skeleton|]] or [[zombie|]]. The summoned creatures appear in unoccupied spaces within 60 feet of the necromancer, whom they obey. They take their turns immediately after the necromancer. Each lasts for 1 hour, until it or the necromancer dies, or until the necromancer dismisses it as a bonus action.


## Reactions

**Grim Harvest (1/Turn).** When the necromancer kills a creature with necrotic damage, the necromancer regains 9 (**2d8**) hit points.
