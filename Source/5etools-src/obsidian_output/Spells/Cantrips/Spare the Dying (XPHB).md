# Spare the Dying

*Cantrip necromancy*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 15 feet  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

Choose a creature within range that has 0 [[Hit Points|XPHB]] and isn't dead. The creature becomes [[Stable|XPHB]].

## Cantrip Upgrade

The range doubles when you reach levels 5 (30 feet), 11 (60 feet), and 17 (120 feet).
