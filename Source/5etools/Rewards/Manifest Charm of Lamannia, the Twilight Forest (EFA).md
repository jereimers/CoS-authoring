# Manifest Charm of Lamannia, the Twilight Forest

*Charm*

When you use a spell slot to cast a spell that conjures or summons a Beast, an Elemental, or a Fey (including [[Conjure Animals|XPHB]], [[Conjure Elemental|XPHB]], [[Conjure Fey|XPHB]], [[Conjure Minor Elementals|XPHB]], [[Conjure Woodland Beings|XPHB]], [[Find Steed|XPHB]], [[Summon Beast|XPHB]], [[Summon Elemental|XPHB]], or [[Summon Fey|XPHB]]), you can expend the Charm (no action required) to treat the spell as if it were cast using a spell slot 1 level above the slot expended.
