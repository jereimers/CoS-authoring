# See Invisibility

*2nd-level divination*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Self  
**Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of talc)  
**Duration:** 1 hour

---

For the duration, you see creatures and objects that have the [[Invisible|XPHB]] condition as if they were visible, and you can see into the Ethereal Plane. Creatures and objects there appear ghostly.

**Source:** 2024 Player's Handbook, p. 314
