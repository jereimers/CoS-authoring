## Traits

**Legendary Resistance (3/Day).** If the death knight fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

**Magic Resistance.** The death knight has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**Marshal Undead.** Undead creatures of the death knight's choice (excluding itself) in a 60-foot [[Emanation [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] originating from it have [[Advantage|XPHB]] on attack rolls and saving throws. It can't use this trait if it has the [[Incapacitated|XPHB]] condition.

**Undead Restoration.** If the death knight is destroyed before it atones for its evil, it gains a new body in `1d10` days, reviving with all its [[Hit Points|XPHB]]. The new body appears in a location significant to the death knight.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The death knight makes three Dread Blade attacks.

**Dread Blade.** m +11, reach 5 ft. @h12 (**2d6 + 5**) Slashing damage plus 13 (**3d8**) Necrotic damage.

**Hellfire Orb {@recharge 5}.** dex DC 18, each creature in a 20-foot-radius [[Sphere [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] centered on a point the death knight can see within 120 feet. @actSaveFail 35 (**10d6**) Fire damage plus 35 (**10d6**) Necrotic damage. @actSaveSuccess Half damage.



## Reactions

**Parry.** @actTrigger The death knight is hit by a melee attack roll while holding a weapon. @actResponse The death knight adds 6 to its AC against that attack, possibly causing it to miss.


## Legendary Actions

The creature can take 3 legendary actions.

**Dread Authority.** The death knight uses Spellcasting to cast [[Command|XPHB]]. The death knight can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.

**Fell Word.** con DC 18, one creature the death knight can see within 120 feet. @actSaveFail 17 (**5d6**) Necrotic damage, and the target's [[Hit Points|XPHB]] maximum decreases by an amount equal to the damage taken. @actSaveSuccessOrFail The death knight can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.

**Lunge.** The death knight moves up to half its [[Speed|XPHB]], and it makes one Dread Blade attack.
