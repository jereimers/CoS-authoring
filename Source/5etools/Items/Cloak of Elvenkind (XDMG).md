# Cloak of Elvenkind

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon* *(requires attunement)*

While you wear this cloak, Wisdom ([[Perception|XPHB]]) checks made to perceive you have [[Disadvantage|XPHB]], and you have [[Advantage|XPHB]] on Dexterity ([[Stealth|XPHB]]) checks.
