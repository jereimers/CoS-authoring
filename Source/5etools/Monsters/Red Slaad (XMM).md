## Actions

**Multiattack.** The slaad makes three Injecting Claw attacks.

**Injecting Claw.** m +6, reach 10 ft. @h10 (**2d6 + 3**) Piercing damage. If the target is a Humanoid not cursed by a slaad, it is subjected to the following effect. con DC 14. @actSaveFail The target is cursed unawares, and a minuscule slaad egg is implanted in it. Removing the curse destroys the egg.

Over `2d4 × 10` days, the egg gestates. In the final 24 hours, the cursed target feels unwell; its [[Speed|XPHB]] is halved, and it has [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on [[D20 Test|XPHB]]. At the end of this time, the egg turns into a Slaad Tadpole, which chews out of the host and kills it.
