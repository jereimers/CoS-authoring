# Eyes of Minute Seeing

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

These crystal lenses fit over the eyes. While wearing them, your vision improves significantly out to a range of 1 foot, granting you [[Darkvision|XPHB]] within that range and [[Advantage|XPHB]] on Intelligence ([[Investigation|XPHB]]) checks made to examine something within that range.
