## Traits

**Magic Resistance.** The altisaur has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The altisaur makes one Stomp attack and one Tail attack. The altisaur can't make both attacks against the same target.

**Stomp.** Melee Weapon Attack +14 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. @h33 (**7d6 + 9**) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Huge or smaller creature, it must succeed on a DC 22 Strength saving throw or have the [[prone]] condition.

**Tail.** Melee Weapon Attack +14 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. @h45 (**8d8 + 9**) bludgeoning damage, and the target is pushed up to 20 feet horizontally from the altisaur.
