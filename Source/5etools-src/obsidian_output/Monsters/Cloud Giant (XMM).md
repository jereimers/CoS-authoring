## Actions

**Multiattack.** The giant makes two attacks, using Thunderous Mace or Thundercloud in any combination. It can replace one attack with a use of Spellcasting to cast [[Fog Cloud|XPHB]].

**Thunderous Mace.** m +12, reach 10 ft. @h21 (**3d8 + 8**) Bludgeoning damage plus 7 (**2d6**) Thunder damage.

**Thundercloud.** r +12, range 240 ft. @h18 (**3d6 + 8**) Thunder damage, and the target has the [[Incapacitated|XPHB]] condition until the end of its next turn.
