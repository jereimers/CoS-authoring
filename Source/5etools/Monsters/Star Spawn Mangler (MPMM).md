## Traits

**Ambusher.** The mangler has advantage on initiative rolls.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The mangler makes two Claw attacks.

**Claw.** Melee Weapon Attack +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h8 (**1d8 + 4**) slashing damage. If the attack roll has advantage, the target also takes 7 (**2d6**) psychic damage.

**Flurry of Claws {@recharge 5}.** The mangler makes six Claw attacks. Either before or after these attacks, it can move up to its speed without provoking [[opportunity attack|]].


## Bonus Actions

**Shadow Stealth.** While in dim light or darkness, the mangler takes the [[Hide]] action.
