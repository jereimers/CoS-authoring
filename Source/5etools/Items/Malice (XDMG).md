# Malice

*Item, None*

A creature subjected to Malice must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or have the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition for 1 hour. The creature also has the [[Blinded|XPHB]] condition while [[Poisoned|XPHB]] in this way.
