# Truth Serum

*Item, None*

A creature subjected to Truth Serum must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or have the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition for 1 hour. The [[Poisoned|XPHB]] creature can't knowingly communicate a lie.
