# Pass without Trace

*2nd-level abjuration*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** {'type': 'emanation', 'distance': {'type': 'feet', 'amount': 30}}  
**Components:** V, S, M (ashes from burned mistletoe)  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

---

You radiate a concealing aura in a 30-foot [[Emanation [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] for the duration. While in the aura, you and each creature you choose have a +10 bonus to Dexterity ([[Stealth|XPHB]]) checks and leave no tracks.
