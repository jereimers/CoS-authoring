# Overview

Those Barovians who fled their village in the aftermath of [[Source/CoS-Reloaded/Chapter 2 - The Land of Barovia/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd's]] siege faced a long and difficult path on the road to [[Town of Vallaki|Vallaki]]. Several lost their lives, whether to wolf attacks or malicious undead, leaving the survivors to bury them along the way—if, of course, there was anything left to bury.

# Denied Entry

When they arrived outside Vallaki's walls and asked for sanctuary, however, they were met with disturbing news: the gates of the town were closed to them. News of Barovia's siege had traveled to Vallaki's streets, and [[Baron Vargas Vallakovich|Baron Vallakovich]] had evidently become convinced that the Barovians themselves were an accursed, unsavory people—and that permitting them to enter his "perfect" town would only empower the Devil to destroy it.

# Current Situation

Most of the refugees despaired, but only a few struck out on the path back to Barovia; those that did were never seen again. The rest linger on the outskirts of Vallaki, huddling in threadbare tents and bedrolls. Their encampment hugs close to Vallaki's palisade walls, praying that the silvered spears and crossbows of the town's guards will keep the beasts of the woods at bay—and that, sooner or later, the people of Vallaki will change their minds and allow them entry.

So far, the wolves have kept their distance, but so have the Vallakians. With resources running low, and morale even lower, the refugees are slowly running out of time—even as the shadows of the woods creep ever-closer to their campfires.
