# Greater Mark of Making

**Prerequisites:** Level 4

You gain the following benefits.

## Improved Intuition

When you use the Artisan's Intuition benefit of your [[Mark of Making|EFA]] feat, you can roll `1d6` instead of `1d4`.

## Improved Making

When you use the Spellsmith benefit of your [[Mark of Making|EFA]] feat to cast [[Magic Weapon|XPHB]] without a spell slot, you cast the spell as its level 3 version.
