# Crusher

**Prerequisites:** Level 4

You gain the following benefits.

## Push

Once per turn, when you hit a creature with an attack that deals Bludgeoning damage, you can move it 5 feet to an unoccupied space if the target is no more than one size larger than you.

## Enhanced Critical

When you score a [[Critical Hit|XPHB]] that deals Bludgeoning damage to a creature, attack rolls against that creature have [[Advantage|XPHB]] until the start of your next turn.
