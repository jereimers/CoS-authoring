# Speedy

**Prerequisites:** Level 4, DEX 13+, Level 4, CON 13+

You gain the following benefits.

## Speed Increase

Your [[Speed|XPHB]] increases by 10 feet.

## Dash over Difficult Terrain

When you take the [[Dash|XPHB]] action on your turn, [[Difficult Terrain|XPHB]] doesn't cost you extra movement for the rest of that turn.

## Agile Movement

[[Opportunity Attack|XPHB]] have [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] against you.
