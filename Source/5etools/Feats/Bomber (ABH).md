# Bomber

**Prerequisites:** Level 4

You gain the following benefits.

## Far Lobber

When you use the [[Attack|XPHB]] action to throw a vial or flask, you can target an object or creature you can see within 40 feet of yourself.

## Long Shots

Attacking at long range doesn't impose [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on your attack rolls with T weapons.
