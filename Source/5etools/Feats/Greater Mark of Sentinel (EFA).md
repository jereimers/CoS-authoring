# Greater Mark of Sentinel

**Prerequisites:** Level 4

You gain the following benefits.

## Improved Intuition

When you use the Sentinel's Intuition benefit of your [[Mark of Sentinel|EFA]] feat, you can roll `1d6` instead of `1d4`.

## Improved Sentinel

When you use the Vigilant Guardian benefit of your [[Mark of Sentinel|EFA]] feat, you can also make one attack with a weapon or an [[Unarmed Strike|XPHB]] as part of that same [[Reaction|XPHB]].
