*Tiny fiend (demon), Chaotic Evil*

**Speed** walk 30 ft., fly 30 ft. (see bad flier below)

**Skills** —

## Traits

**Bad Flier.** The abyssal chicken falls at the end of a turn if it's airborne and the only thing holding it aloft is its flying speed.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The abyssal chicken makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

**Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. @h4 (**1d4 + 2**) piercing damage.

**Claws.** Melee Weapon Attack +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h5 (**1d6 + 2**) slashing damage.
