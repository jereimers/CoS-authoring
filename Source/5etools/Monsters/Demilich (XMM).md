## Traits

**Legendary Resistance (3/Day, or 4/Day in Lair).** If the demilich fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

**Undead Restoration.** If the demilich is destroyed, it reforms and regains all its [[Hit Points|XPHB]] in `1d10` days unless a [[Wish|XPHB]] spell is cast on its remains.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The demilich makes three Necrotic Burst attacks.

**Necrotic Burst.** m,r +11, reach 5 ft. or range 120 ft. @h24 (**7d6**) Necrotic damage.

**Howl {@recharge 5}.** con DC 19, each creature in a 30-foot [[Emanation [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] originating from the demilich. @actSaveFail 70 (**20d6**) Psychic damage. @actSaveSuccessOrFail The target has the [[Frightened|XPHB]] condition until the start of the demilich's next turn.




## Legendary Actions

The creature can take 3 legendary actions.

**Energy Drain.** con DC 19, one creature the demilich can see within 120 feet. @actSaveFail The target's [[Hit Points|XPHB]] maximum decreases by 14 (`4d6`). @actSaveSuccessOrFail The demilich can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.

**Grave-Dust Flight.** The demilich flies up to its [[Fly Speed|XPHB]], shedding grave dust. Each creature within 5 feet of the demilich as it moves is targeted once by the following effect. con DC 19. @actSaveFail The target has the [[Blinded|XPHB]] condition until the end of the demilich's next turn. @actSaveSuccessOrFail The demilich can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.

**Necrosis.** The demilich makes one Necrotic Burst attack.
