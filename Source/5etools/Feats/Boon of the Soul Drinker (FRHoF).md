# Boon of the Soul Drinker

**Prerequisites:** Level 19

You gain the following benefits.

## Grave Resistance

You have [[Resistance|XPHB]] to Cold damage and Necrotic damage.

## Siphon Life

When an enemy within 120 feet of you is reduced to 0 [[Hit Points|XPHB]], you can take a [[Reaction|XPHB]] to regain 50 [[Hit Points|XPHB]]. Once you use this benefit, you can't use it again until you finish a [[Short Rest|XPHB]] or [[Long Rest|XPHB]].
