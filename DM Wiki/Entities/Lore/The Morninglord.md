# Overview

The Morninglord is a god of dawn and renewal, the central deity worshipped by the [[Church of the Morninglord]] throughout [[Barovia]].

# The Faith's Decline

In centuries past, with the sun always hidden behind overcast skies, the people's faith in the Morninglord had slowly slipped into empty ritual. Before the curse of [[Source/CoS-Reloaded/Chapter 2 - The Land of Barovia/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] befell the land, the Morninglord watched over the Barovian people from sunrise until sundown. Now, the sun has not shone unobscured for centuries, and the Morninglord no longer answers their prayers.

# Recent Revival

With [[Source/CoS-Reloaded/Chapter 2 - The Land of Barovia/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]] newly awoken, however, a wave of despair has swept across the valley—and, with it, a streak of religious revivalism. [[Town of Vallaki|Vallaki]] in particular has seen a dramatic expansion in worshippers seeking hope, comfort, and a sense of security from the faith of their forefathers.

# True Identity

> [!info]+ The Names of the Gods
> In *Forgotten Realms* lore, "Morninglord" is the epithet of Lathander, the greater god of dawn and renewal.
