## Traits

**Diabolical Restoration.** If the devil dies outside the Nine Hells, its body disappears in sulfurous smoke, and it gains a new body instantly, reviving with all its [[Hit Points|XPHB]] somewhere in the Nine Hells.

**Magic Resistance.** The devil has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The devil makes two Claw attacks and one Infernal Sting attack.

**Claw.** m +8, reach 10 ft. @h13 (**2d8 + 4**) Slashing damage.

**Infernal Sting.** m +8, reach 10 ft. @h15 (**2d10 + 4**) Piercing damage plus 18 (**4d8**) Poison damage, and the target has the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition until the start of the devil's next turn. While [[Poisoned|XPHB]], the target can't regain [[Hit Points|XPHB]].
