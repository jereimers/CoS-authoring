# Prying Blade

*Item, Unknown (Magic)*

A hooked blade useful on board a ship or as a cutting tool in the wilderness, this [[+1 shortsword]] grants its wielder advantage on Strength ([[Athletics]]) checks to climb or to escape while [[restrained]].
