# Boon of Skill

**Prerequisites:** Level 19

You gain the following benefits.

## All-Around Adept

You gain proficiency in all skills.

## Expertise

Choose one skill in which you lack [[Expertise|XPHB]]. You gain [[Expertise|XPHB]] in that skill.
