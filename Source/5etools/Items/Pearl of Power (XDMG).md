# Pearl of Power

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon* *(requires attunement by a spellcaster)*

While this pearl is on your person, you can take a [[Magic|XPHB]] action to regain one expended spell slot of level 3 or lower. Once you use the pearl, it can't be used again until the next dawn.
