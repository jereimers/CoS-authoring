# Necklace of Adaptation

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon* *(requires attunement)*

While wearing this necklace, you can breathe normally in any environment, and you have [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws made to avoid or end the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition.
