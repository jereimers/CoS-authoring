## Traits

**Amphibious.** Tecuziztecatl can breathe air and water.

**Glowing.** Tecuziztecatl sheds dim light within 20 feet of itself.

**Pseudopod.** Melee Weapon Attack +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. @h12 (**2d8 + 3**) bludgeoning damage.

**Spit Acid {@recharge 4}.** Tecuziztecatl exhales acid in a 30-foot line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 18 (**4d8**) acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
