## Traits

**Immutable Form.** The gearkeeper is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.

**Rapid Shifting.** Opportunity attacks made against the gearkeeper have disadvantage.

**Whirling Blades.** Any creature that starts its turn within 5 feet of the gearkeeper takes 4 (**1d8**) slashing damage.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The gearkeeper makes two Arm Blade attacks, or one Arm Blade attack and one Spear Launcher attack.

**Arm Blade.** Melee Weapon Attack +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h18 (**3d8 + 5**) slashing damage.

**Spear Launcher.** Ranged Weapon Attack +9 to hit, range 90 ft., one target. @h12 (**2d6 + 5**) piercing damage, and the target is knocked [[prone]].

**Shrapnel Blast {@recharge}.** The gearkeeper jettisons a spray of jagged metal in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in the area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 21 (**6d6**) piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
