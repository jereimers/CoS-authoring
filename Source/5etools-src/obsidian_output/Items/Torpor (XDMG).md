# Torpor

*Item, None*

A creature subjected to Torpor poison must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or have the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition for `4d6` hours. The creature's [[Speed|XPHB]] is halved while the creature is [[Poisoned|XPHB]] in this way.
