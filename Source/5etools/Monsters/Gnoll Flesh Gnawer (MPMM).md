## Actions

**Multiattack.** The gnoll makes one Bite attack and two Shortsword attacks.

**Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h4 (**1d4 + 2**) piercing damage.

**Shortsword.** Melee Weapon Attack +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h5 (**1d6 + 2**) piercing damage.

**Sudden Rush.** Until the end of the turn, the gnoll's speed increases by 60 feet and it doesn't provoke [[opportunity attack|]].


## Bonus Actions

**Rampage.** After the gnoll reduces a creature to 0 hit points with a melee attack on its turn, the gnoll moves up to half its speed and makes a Bite attack.
