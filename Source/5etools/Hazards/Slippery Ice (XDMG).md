# Slippery Ice

Slippery ice is [[Difficult Terrain|XPHB]]. A creature that moves onto slippery ice for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity saving throw or have the [[Prone|XPHB]] condition.
