# Who they are

Stanimir is the "papa" of the [[Vistani]] encampment at [[Tser Pool Encampment|Tser Pool]], a respected elder who serves as a welcoming host to travelers. He is a skilled storyteller and keeper of [[Vistani]] traditions, including the Game of Stories—a wagering game where participants share tales and others must guess whether they are true, false, or half-true.

# What the party knows

- Stanimir is the leader ("papa") of the [[Tser Pool Encampment]]
- He invited the party to join the [[Vistani]] for dinner and to play the Game of Stories
- He shared [[Stanimir's Tale]]—the story of a wounded prince (later revealed to be [[Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd]]) whom the [[Vistani]] nursed back to health centuries ago, earning his eternal gratitude and safe passage in his lands
- He thanked [[Arden Nalero|Arden]] for making morning coffee (though he noted it tasted "a bit like dust")
- He prepared breakfast for the camp

# Personality

- Warm and hospitable, treating guests like family
- A master storyteller who uses fire magic to illustrate his tales
- Proud of [[Vistani]] traditions and history
- Maintains a cheerful demeanor even in dark times
