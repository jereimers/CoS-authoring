# Grappler

**Prerequisites:** Level 4, STR 13+, Level 4, DEX 13+

You gain the following benefits.

## Punch and Grab

When you hit a creature with an [[Unarmed Strike|XPHB]] as part of the [[Attack|XPHB]] action on your turn, you can use both the Damage and the Grapple option. You can use this benefit only once per turn.

## Attack Advantage

You have [[Advantage|XPHB]] on attack rolls against a creature [[Grappled|XPHB]] by you.

## Fast Wrestler

You don't have to spend extra movement to move a creature [[Grappled|XPHB]] by you if the creature is your size or smaller.
