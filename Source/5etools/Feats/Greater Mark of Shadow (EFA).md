# Greater Mark of Shadow

**Prerequisites:** Level 4

You gain the following benefits.

## Improved Intuition

When you use the Cunning Intuition benefit of your [[Mark of Shadow|EFA]] feat, you can roll `1d6` instead of `1d4`.

## Improved Shadow

When you use the Shape Shadows benefit of your [[Mark of Shadow|EFA]] feat to cast [[Invisibility|XPHB]] without a spell slot, you cast the spell as its level 3 version.
