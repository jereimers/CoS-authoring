# Elixir of Health

*Item, Rare*

When you drink this potion, you are cured of all magical contagions. In addition, the following conditions end on you: [[Blinded|XPHB]], [[Deafened|XPHB]], [[Paralyzed|XPHB]], and [[Poisoned|XPHB]].

The clear, red liquid has tiny bubbles of light in it.
