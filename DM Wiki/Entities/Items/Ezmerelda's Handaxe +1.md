# Ezmerelda's Handaxe +1

## Description
A **+1 handaxe** carried by [[Ezmerelda d'Avenir]], the Vistana vampire hunter. This versatile weapon can be used both in melee combat and thrown at range, making it an excellent backup weapon for a monster hunter who must be prepared for any situation.

## Properties
- **Type:** Simple Melee Weapon
- **Damage:** 1d6 + 3 slashing (including +1 magical bonus and Strength modifier)
- **Attack Bonus:** +6 to hit
- **Properties:** Light, Thrown (range 20/60 ft.)
- **Rarity:** Uncommon
- **Attunement:** No

## Combat Statistics
As wielded by Ezmerelda:
- **Melee Attack:** +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target
- **Ranged Attack:** +6 to hit, range 20/60 ft., one target
- **Hit:** 6 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage
