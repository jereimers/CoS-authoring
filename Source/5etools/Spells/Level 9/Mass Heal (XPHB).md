# Mass Heal

*9th-level abjuration*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 60 feet  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

A flood of healing energy flows from you into creatures around you. You restore up to 700 [[Hit Points|XPHB]], divided as you choose among any number of creatures that you can see within range. Creatures healed by this spell also have the [[Blinded|XPHB]], [[Deafened|XPHB]], and [[Poisoned|XPHB]] conditions removed from them.
