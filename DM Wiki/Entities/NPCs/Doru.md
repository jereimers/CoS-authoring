## Roleplaying Information

### Resonance
Doru should inspire sympathy for his struggles against his vampiric nature, mild discomfort with his occasionally inhuman mannerisms, and endearment for his clear dedication to his father and Gertruda.

### Emotions
Doru most often feels grief, guilt, shame, desperation, resignation, and hope.

### Motivations
Doru wants to reconcile with his father and ensure Gertruda's safety.

### Inspirations
