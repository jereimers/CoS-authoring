**Appearance:** A tall (6 ft), athletic drow male with white hair, black skin, and striking purple eyes. At over three centuries old, Arden has seen much of the world—and much of its darkness.

**Key Abilities:** A powerful frontline warrior wielding divine vengeance against evil. Immune to disease and magical sleep, with advantage against being charmed. His Oath of Vengeance grants him the ability to mark enemies with his Vow of Enmity and Abjure Enemy. His Haunted One background means commoners sense the horrors he has faced and will rally to his cause.

# Backstory (public)

Arden has faced unimaginable horror in his long life—something that has left its mark in his purple eyes for all to see. Though he is a drow, a people often feared and reviled, he has dedicated himself to the path of vengeance against evil and the protection of the common folk. His centuries of experience have made him meticulous in all things, and he trusts in the guidance of the divine to show him the way.
