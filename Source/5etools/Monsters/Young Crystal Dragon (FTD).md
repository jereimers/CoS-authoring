## Actions

**Multiattack.** The dragon makes one Bite attack and two Claw attacks.

**Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. @h14 (**2d10 + 3**) piercing damage plus 4 (**1d8**) radiant damage.

**Claw.** Melee Weapon Attack +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h6 (**1d6 + 3**) slashing damage.

**Scintillating Breath {@recharge 5}.** The dragon exhales a burst of brilliant radiance in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 27 (**6d8**) radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The dragon then gains 10 temporary hit points by absorbing a portion of the radiant energy.
