# Mass Healing Word

*3rd-level abjuration*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 bonus  
**Range:** 60 feet  
**Components:** V  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

Up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range regain [[Hit Points|XPHB]] equal to `2d4` plus your spellcasting ability modifier.

## Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot

The healing increases by `2d4` for each spell slot level above 3.
