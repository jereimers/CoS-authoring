# Boon of Dimensional Travel

**Prerequisites:** Level 19

You gain the following benefits.

## Blink Steps

Immediately after you take the [[Attack|XPHB]] action or the [[Magic|XPHB]] action, you can teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see.
