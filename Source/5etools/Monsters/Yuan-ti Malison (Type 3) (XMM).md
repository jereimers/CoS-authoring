## Traits

**Magic Resistance.** The yuan-ti has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The yuan-ti makes two Poison Burst attacks, and it can use Spellcasting to cast [[Suggestion|XPHB]] if available.

**Poison Burst (Yuan-ti Form Only).** m,r +5, reach 5 ft. or range 120 ft. @h12 (**2d8 + 3**) Poison damage.

**Constrict.** str DC 13, one Medium or smaller creature within 5 feet. @actSaveFail 21 (**4d8 + 3**) Bludgeoning damage. The target has the [[Grappled|XPHB]] condition (escape DC 13), and it has the [[Restrained|XPHB]] condition until the grapple ends.


## Bonus Actions

**Shape-Shift.** The yuan-ti shape-shifts into a Medium snake or returns to its true form. If it dies, it stays in its current form. The yuan-ti's game statistics are the same in each form, except where noted. Any equipment it is wearing or carrying isn't transformed.
