# Overview

The Tser Pool Encampment is a [[Vistani]] camp situated along the Tser River, downstream from [[Tser Falls]]. This calm body of water provides a peaceful resting place for travelers, and the [[Vistani]] here offer hospitality to those passing through. The camp is led by [[Stanimir]] ("Papa") and is also home to the ancient seer [[Madam Eva]], whose tent sits at the edge of the encampment.

# What the party knows

- The camp is a safe haven where the [[Vistani]] offer hospitality to travelers
- [[Stanimir]] serves as the camp's "papa" or leader
- [[Madam Eva]], the renowned Vistani seer, resides here in her own tent
- The camp hosts the Game of Stories, a wagering game where participants share tales
- [[Arturi Radanavich]] and [[Eliza]] were guests at the camp when the party arrived
- The party spent an evening here sharing stories around the campfire, eating mutton stew, and drinking Barovian wine

# Notable places

- [[Madam Eva]]'s tent
- The central campfire where the Game of Stories was played
- The calm pool of the Tser River

# Notable figures

- [[Arturi Radanavich]] - A cursed Vistani traveler
