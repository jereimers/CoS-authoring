## Legendary Actions

The creature can take 3 legendary actions.

**Cloaked Flight.** The dragon uses Spellcasting to cast [[Invisibility|XPHB]] on itself, and it can fly up to half its [[Fly Speed|XPHB]]. The dragon can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.

**Sonic Boom.** The dragon uses Spellcasting to cast [[Shatter|XPHB]]. The dragon can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.

**Tail Swipe.** The dragon makes one Rend attack.
