# Redface

The effects of redface are identical to those of [[sight rot]] (see "[[Diseases|DMG]]" in chapter 8 of the [[Dungeon Master's Guide|DMG]]), but it's caused by pollutants in the air rather than contaminated drinking water, making it difficult to avoid.
