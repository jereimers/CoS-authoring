## Traits

**Demonic Restoration.** If the demon dies outside the Abyss, its body dissolves into ichor, and it gains a new body instantly, reviving with all its [[Hit Points|XPHB]] somewhere in the Abyss.

**Incorporeal Movement.** The demon can move through other creatures and objects as if they were [[Difficult Terrain|XPHB]]. It takes 5 (**1d10**) Force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.

**Light Sensitivity.** While in [[Bright Light|XPHB]], the demon has [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on ability checks and attack rolls.

**Shadow Stealth.** While in [[Dim Light|XPHB]] or [[Darkness|XPHB]], the demon takes the Hide action.
