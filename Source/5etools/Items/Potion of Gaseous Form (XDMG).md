# Potion of Gaseous Form

*Item, Rare*

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [[Gaseous Form|XPHB]] spell for 1 hour (no [[Concentration]] required) or until you end the effect as a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]].

This potion's container seems to hold fog that moves and pours like water.
