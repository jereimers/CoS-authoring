# Rebuke

**Prerequisites:** Level 4

You gain the following benefits.

## Radiant Strike

When you make a damage roll that deals Radiant damage, you can cause one Huge or smaller creature taking the damage to have the [[Prone|XPHB]] condition. Once you use this benefit, you can't use it again until you finish a [[Short Rest|XPHB]] or [[Long Rest|XPHB]].
