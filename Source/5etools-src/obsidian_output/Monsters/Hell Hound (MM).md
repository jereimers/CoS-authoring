## Traits

**Keen Hearing and Smell.** The hound has advantage on Wisdom ([[Perception]]) checks that rely on hearing or smell.

**Pack Tactics.** The hound has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the hound's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't [[incapacitated]].


## Actions

**Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h7 (**1d8 + 3**) piercing damage plus 7 (**2d6**) fire damage.

**Fire Breath {@recharge 5}.** The hound exhales fire in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking 21 (**6d6**) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
