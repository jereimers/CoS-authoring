# Overview

In Soldav, the hidden bastion of the [[Mountain Folk]], the people still keep to the old faith of the Ladies of the Fanes: the Weaver, the Seeker, and the Huntress. They do their best to honor their goddesses with offerings of food, flowers, and song, but mourn the loss of knowledge and tradition that came when the druids betrayed their faith for [[Source/CoS-Reloaded/Chapter 2 - The Land of Barovia/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd's]] protection.

# Lost Knowledge

They no longer remember the sacred rites that once bound their ancestors to the Ladies' fellowship, nor do they remember the secrets of the Ladies' blessings.

# What They Remember

The [[Mountain Folk]] still remember, though, that:

## The Sacred Places

- The **Weaver's** holy place was the Swamp Fane, near the ruined town on the shores of the Luna River
- The **Huntress's** holy place was the Forest Fane, atop [[Yester Hill]]
- The **Seeker's** holy place was the Mountain Fane, in a western clearing in the valley's woodland basin

## The Three Goddesses

### The Seeker

The Seeker was a goddess of foresight, prophecy, and storms, her sigil a raven's eye, ever-searching.

### The Weaver

The Weaver was a goddess of resilience, craftsmanship, and care, her sigil a spider's delicate web.

### The Huntress

The Huntress was a goddess of beasts, wild places, and war, her sigil a sharpened wolf's tooth.

## The Divine Trinity

Together, this trinity oversaw each life that was born in the valley:
- The **Seeker**, forging the threads of fate that would bind them together
- The **Weaver**, spinning those threads into a lush and vibrant tapestry
- The **Huntress**, cutting each thread at its appointed time, and ushering its soul into death
