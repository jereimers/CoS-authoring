# Overview

The people of Krezk are a reclusive community living in the fortified village at the western end of the valley, built around an old abbey. They are led by burgomaster Dmitri Krezkov.

# The Secret

The villagers are largely unaware that their burgomaster, Dmitri Krezkov, is secretly a werewolf.
