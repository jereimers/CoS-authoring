# Burning

A burning creature or object takes **1d4** Fire damage at the start of each of its turns. As an action, you can extinguish fire on yourself by giving yourself the [[Prone|XPHB]] condition and rolling on the ground. The fire also goes out if it is doused, submerged, or suffocated.
