## Traits

**Immutable Form.** The colossus can't shape-shift.

**Legendary Resistance (4/Day).** If the colossus fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

**Magic Resistance.** The colossus has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**Siege Monster.** The colossus deals double damage to objects and structures.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The colossus makes three attacks, using Slam or Radiant Ray in any combination.

**Slam.** m +18, reach 20 ft. @h32 (**4d10 + 10**) Bludgeoning damage, and the colossus pushes the target up to 20 feet straight away from itself.

**Radiant Ray.** r +18, range 300 ft. @h22 (**4d10**) Radiant damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it has the [[Prone|XPHB]] condition.

**Divine Beam {@recharge 5}.** dex DC 26, each creature in a 300-foot-long, 10-foot-wide [[Line [Area of Effect]|XPHB]]. @actSaveFail 65 (**10d12**) Radiant damage. @actSaveSuccess Half damage. @actSaveSuccessOrFail A creature reduced to 0 [[Hit Points|XPHB]] by this beam disintegrates into dust, leaving behind any magic items it was wearing or carrying.




## Legendary Actions

The creature can take 3 legendary actions.

**Smite.** The colossus makes one Radiant Ray attack.

**Stomp.** The colossus moves up to half its [[Speed|XPHB]] without provoking [[Opportunity Attack|XPHB]], and it can make one Slam attack at any point during that move.
