# Detect Evil and Good

*1st-level divination*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Self (30-feet sphere)  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

---

For the duration, you sense the location of any Aberration, Celestial, Elemental, Fey, Fiend, or Undead within 30 feet of yourself. You also sense whether the [[Hallow|XPHB]] spell is active there and, if so, where.

The spell is blocked by 1 foot of stone, dirt, or wood; 1 inch of metal; or a thin sheet of lead.
