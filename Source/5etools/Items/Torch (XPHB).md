# Torch

*Item, None*

A Torch burns for 1 hour, casting [[Bright Light|XPHB]] in a 20-foot radius and [[Dim Light|XPHB]] for an additional 20 feet. When you take the [[Attack|XPHB]] action, you can attack with the Torch, using it as a Simple Melee weapon. On a hit, the target takes 1 Fire damage.
