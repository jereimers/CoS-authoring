# Potion of Heroism

*Item, Rare*

When you drink this potion, you gain 10 [[Temporary Hit Points|XPHB]] that last for 1 hour. For the same duration, you are under the effect of the [[Bless|XPHB]] spell (no [[Concentration]] required).

This potion's blue liquid bubbles and steams as if boiling.
