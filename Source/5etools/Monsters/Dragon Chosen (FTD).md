## Actions

**Multiattack.** The chosen makes one Handaxe attack and two Shortsword attacks.

**Handaxe.** Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. @h7 (**1d6 + 4**) slashing damage. @homThe handaxe magically returns to the chosen's hand immediately after a ranged attack.

**Shortsword.** Melee Weapon Attack +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h7 (**1d6 + 4**) piercing damage.



## Reactions

**Biting Rebuke.** Immediately after the chosen takes damage from a creature within 5 feet of it, it can make a Shortsword attack with advantage against that creature.
