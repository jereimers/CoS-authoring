# Enthrall

*2nd-level enchantment*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 60 feet  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** 1 minute

---

You weave a distracting string of words, causing creatures of your choice that you can see within range to make a Wisdom saving throw. Any creature you or your companions are fighting automatically succeeds on this save. On a failed save, a target has a -10 penalty to Wisdom ([[Perception|XPHB]]) checks and Passive [[Perception|XPHB]] until the spell ends.
