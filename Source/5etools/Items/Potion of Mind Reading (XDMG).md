# Potion of Mind Reading

*Item, Rare*

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [[Detect Thoughts|XPHB]] spell (save DC 13) for 10 minutes (no [[Concentration]] required).

This potion's dense, purple liquid has an ovoid cloud of pink floating in it.
