# Zhentarim Ruffian

You gain the following benefits.

## Exploit Opening

When you roll damage for an [[Opportunity Attack|XPHB]], you can roll the damage dice twice and use either roll against the target.

## Family First

If you have [[Heroic Inspiration|XPHB]] when you roll [[Initiative|XPHB]], you can expend it to give yourself and your allies [[Advantage|XPHB]] on that [[Initiative|XPHB]] roll.
