# Lorwyn Changeling

**Size:** Small, Medium  
**Speed:** 30  

## Shape Self

As an action, you can reshape your body to a two-legged Humanoid shape or to a four-legged Beast shape. While you have a Humanoid shape, you can wear clothing and armor made for a Humanoid of your size.

## Darkvision

You have [[Darkvision|XPHB]] with a range of 120 feet.

## Delightful Imitator

You have proficiency in the [[Performance|XPHB]] skill.

## Unpredictable Movement

When you roll [[Initiative|XPHB]] and don't have [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on that roll, you can take the [[Dash|XPHB]] action as a [[Reaction|XPHB]].
