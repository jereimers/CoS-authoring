# Bracers of Archery

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon* *(requires attunement)*

While wearing these bracers, you have proficiency with the [[Longbow|XPHB]] and [[Shortbow|XPHB]], and you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls made with such weapons.
