## Traits

**Undead Restoration.** If the revenant dies, it revives 24 hours later unless [[Dispel Evil and Good|XPHB]] is cast on its remains. If it revives, it animates another group of corpses elsewhere on the same plane of existence; it now looks different but uses the same stat block and returns with all its [[Hit Points|XPHB]].
