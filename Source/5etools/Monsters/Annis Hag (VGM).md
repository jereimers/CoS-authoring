## Actions

**Multiattack.** The annis makes three attacks: one with her bite and two with her claws.

**Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h15 (**3d6 + 5**) piercing damage.

**Claw.** Melee Weapon Attack +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h15 (**3d6 + 5**) slashing damage.

**Crushing Hug.** Melee Weapon Attack +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h36 (**9d6 + 5**) bludgeoning damage, and the target is [[grappled]] (escape DC 15) if it is a Large or smaller creature. Until the grapple ends, the target takes 36 (**9d6 + 5**) bludgeoning damage at the start of each of the hag's turns. The hag can't make attacks while grappling a creature in this way.
