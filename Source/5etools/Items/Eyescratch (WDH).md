# Eyescratch

*Item, None*

A creature that comes into contact with this poison must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be [[poisoned]] for 1 hour and [[blinded]] while [[poisoned]] in this way. A [[lesser restoration]] spell or similar magic ends the effect.
