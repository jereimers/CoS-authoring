**Spider Climb.** If the swarm has a [[Climb Speed|XPHB]], the swarm can climb difficult surfaces, including along ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

**Swarm.** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny insect. The swarm can't regain [[Hit Points|XPHB]] or gain [[Temporary Hit Points|XPHB]].
