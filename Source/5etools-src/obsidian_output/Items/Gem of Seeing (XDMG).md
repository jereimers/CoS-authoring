# Gem of Seeing

*Wondrous Item, Rare* *(requires attunement)*

This gem has 3 charges. As a [[Magic|XPHB]] action, you can expend 1 charge. For the next 10 minutes, you have [[Truesight|XPHB]] out to 120 feet when you peer through the gem.

The gem regains `1d3` expended charges daily at dawn.
