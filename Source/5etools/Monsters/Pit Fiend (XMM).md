**Diabolical Restoration.** If the pit fiend dies outside the Nine Hells, its body disappears in sulfurous smoke, and it gains a new body instantly, reviving with all its [[Hit Points|XPHB]] somewhere in the Nine Hells.

**Fear Aura.** The pit fiend emanates an aura in a 20-foot [[Emanation [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] while it doesn't have the [[Incapacitated|XPHB]] condition. wis DC 21, any enemy that starts its turn in the aura. @actSaveFail The target has the [[Frightened|XPHB]] condition until the start of its next turn. @actSaveSuccess The target is immune to this pit fiend's aura for 24 hours.

**Magic Resistance.** The pit fiend has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
