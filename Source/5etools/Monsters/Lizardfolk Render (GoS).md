## Traits

**Blood Frenzy.** The render has advantage on melee attack rolls against any creature that doesn't have all its hit points.

## Actions

**Multiattack.** The render makes two attacks: one with its claws and one with its bite.

**Claws.** Melee Weapon Attack +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. @h12 (**2d8 + 3**) slashing damage.

**Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h8 (**1d10 + 3**) piercing damage.

**Rend the Field {@recharge 5}.** The render makes a claw attack against each creature of its choice within 10 feet of it. A creature hit by this attack must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked [[prone]].
