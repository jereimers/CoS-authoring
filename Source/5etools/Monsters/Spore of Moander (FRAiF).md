**Explosive Core.** When the spore is subjected to Fire damage, each creature in a 5-foot [[Emanation [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] originating from the spore takes 7 (**2d6**) Fire damage.

**Rolling Mass.** The spore doesn't need to expend extra movement to move through [[Difficult Terrain|XPHB]].
