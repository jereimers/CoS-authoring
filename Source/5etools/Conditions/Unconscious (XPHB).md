# Unconscious

While you have the Unconscious condition, you experience the following effects.

### Inert

You have the [[Incapacitated|XPHB]] and [[Prone|XPHB]] conditions, and you drop whatever you're holding. When this condition ends, you remain [[Prone|XPHB]].

### Speed 0

Your [[Speed|XPHB]] is 0 and can't increase.

### Attacks Affected

[[Attack|XPHB]] rolls against you have [[Advantage|XPHB]].

### Saving Throws Affected

You automatically fail Strength and Dexterity [[Saving Throw|XPHB]].

### Automatic Critical Hits

Any attack roll that hits you is a [[Critical Hit|XPHB]] if the attacker is within 5 feet of you.

### Unaware

You're unaware of your surroundings.
