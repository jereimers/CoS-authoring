## Actions

**Multiattack.** The mummy makes two Rotting Fist attacks and uses Dreadful Glare.

**Rotting Fist.** m +5, reach 5 ft. @h8 (**1d10 + 3**) Bludgeoning damage plus 10 (**3d6**) Necrotic damage. If the target is a creature, it is cursed. While cursed, the target can't regain [[Hit Points|XPHB]], its [[Hit Points|XPHB]] maximum doesn't return to normal when finishing a [[Long Rest|XPHB]], and its [[Hit Points|XPHB]] maximum decreases by 10 (`3d6`) every 24 hours that elapse. A creature dies and turns to dust if reduced to 0 [[Hit Points|XPHB]] by this attack.

**Dreadful Glare.** wis DC 11, one creature the mummy can see within 60 feet. @actSaveFail The target has the [[Frightened|XPHB]] condition until the end of the mummy's next turn. @actSaveSuccess The target is immune to this mummy's Dreadful Glare for 24 hours.
