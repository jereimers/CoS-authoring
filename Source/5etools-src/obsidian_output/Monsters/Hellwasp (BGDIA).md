## Actions

**Multiattack.** The hellwasp makes two attacks: one with its sting and one with its sword talons.

**Sting.** Melee Weapon Attack +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. @h8 (**1d8 + 4**) piercing damage plus 7 (**2d6**) fire damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or be [[poisoned]] for 1 minute. While [[poisoned]] in this way, the target is also [[paralyzed]]. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

**Sword Talons.** Melee Weapon Attack +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h11 (**2d6 + 4**) piercing damage.
