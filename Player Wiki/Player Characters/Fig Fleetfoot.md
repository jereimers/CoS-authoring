**Appearance:** A small (3.5 ft) harengon with grey fur and distinctive white patches around their eyes, nose, tail, and ears. Their light lavender eyes carry a weight that speaks of darkness witnessed. 

**Key Abilities:** A swift and agile martial artist with exceptional dexterity. Fig's Haunted One background means that commoners sense the horrors they've faced and will extend them every courtesy—even taking up arms alongside them against enemies.

# Backstory (public)

Fig carries the weight of something terrible in their past—something they refuse to speak about. Whatever horror they witnessed has left an indelible mark on their soul, visible to anyone who looks into their eyes. They travel now seeking redemption for a guilt that consumes them, hoping that through their actions they might find some measure of peace.

# Personality & Beliefs

**Personality Traits:**
