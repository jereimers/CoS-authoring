# Blur

*2nd-level illusion*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Self  
**Components:** V  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

---

Your body becomes blurred. For the duration, any creature has [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on attack rolls against you. An attacker is immune to this effect if it perceives you with [[Blindsight|XPHB]] or [[Truesight|XPHB]].
