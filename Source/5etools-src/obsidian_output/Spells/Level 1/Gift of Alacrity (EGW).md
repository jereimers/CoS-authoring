# Gift of Alacrity

*1st-level divination*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 minute  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** 8 hours

---

You touch a willing creature. For the duration, the target can add `1d8` to its initiative rolls.
