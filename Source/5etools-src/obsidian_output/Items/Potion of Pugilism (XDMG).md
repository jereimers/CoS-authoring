# Potion of Pugilism

*Item, Uncommon*

After you drink this potion, each [[Unarmed Strike|XPHB]] you make deals an extra **1d6** Force damage on a hit. This effect lasts 10 minutes.

This potion is a thick green fluid that tastes like spinach.
