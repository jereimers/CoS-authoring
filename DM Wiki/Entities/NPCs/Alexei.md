# Who They Are

Alexei is a young Vistani man living at the [[Vistani Camp]] outside [[Town of Vallaki|Vallaki]]. He is [[Arabelle]]'s cousin and was entrusted with watching over her while [[Luvash]], her father, was occupied with camp business.

# What the Party Knows

- Alexei was watching Arabelle when she disappeared
- He fell into a deep, drunken sleep after being given wine by [[Yan]], a fellow Vistani
- When he awoke, both Arabelle and Yan were gone
- Luvash was furious with Alexei for failing to protect his daughter
- The party witnessed Luvash's rage directed at Alexei when they arrived at the [[Vistani Camp]] in [[Session 13 - The Lost and The Found|Session 13]]
