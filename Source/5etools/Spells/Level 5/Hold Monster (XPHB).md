# Hold Monster

*5th-level enchantment*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 90 feet  
**Components:** V, S, M (a straight piece of iron)  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

---

Choose a creature that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the [[Paralyzed|XPHB]] condition for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target repeats the save, ending the spell on itself on a success.

## Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot

You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 5.
