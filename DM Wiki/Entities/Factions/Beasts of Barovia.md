# Overview

While not members of any civilized society, the wolves, rats, and bats of [[Barovia]] are [[Source/CoS-Reloaded/Chapter 2 - The Land of Barovia/Strahd von Zarovich|Strahd's]] loyal servants and spies.

# The Daily Reports

Each day, at sunrise and sunset, each beast with news to report—of outsiders, of turmoil, or simply of interesting trinkets or treasures—returns to [[Castle Ravenloft]] to share what it's learned and to receive new orders to disseminate through the valley.

# Organization

Strahd's vampiric brides gather and organize their intelligence:
- The **wolves** report to [[Volenta Popofsky]]
- The **rats** report to Ludmilla Vilisevic
- The **bats** report to Anastrasya Karelova
