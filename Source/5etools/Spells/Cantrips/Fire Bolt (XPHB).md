# Fire Bolt

*Cantrip evocation*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 120 feet  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

You hurl a mote of fire at a creature or an object within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes **1d10** Fire damage. A flammable object hit by this spell starts [[burning|XPHB]] if it isn't being worn or carried.

## Cantrip Upgrade

The damage increases by **1d10** when you reach levels 5 (**2d10**), 11 (**3d10**), and 17 (**4d10**).
