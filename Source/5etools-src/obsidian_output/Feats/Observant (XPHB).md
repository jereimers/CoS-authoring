# Observant

**Prerequisites:** Level 4, INT 13+, Level 4, WIS 13+

You gain the following benefits.

## Keen Observer

Choose one of the following skills: [[Insight|XPHB]], [[Investigation|XPHB]], or [[Perception|XPHB]]. If you lack proficiency with the chosen skill, you gain proficiency in it, and if you already have proficiency in it, you gain [[Expertise|XPHB]] in it.

## Quick Search

You can take the [[Search|XPHB]] action as a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]].
