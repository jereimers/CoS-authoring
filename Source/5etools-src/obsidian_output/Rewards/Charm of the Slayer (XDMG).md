# Charm of the Slayer

*Charm*

One weapon in your possession becomes a [[Dragon Slayer|XDMG]] or [[Giant Slayer|XDMG]] (DM's choice) for the next 9 days. The Charm then vanishes from you, and the weapon returns to normal.
