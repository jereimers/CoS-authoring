# Ring of Evasion

*Item, Rare* *(requires attunement)*

This ring has 3 charges, and it regains `1d3` expended charges daily at dawn. When you fail a Dexterity saving throw while wearing the ring, you can take a [[Reaction|XPHB]] to expend 1 charge to succeed on that save instead.
