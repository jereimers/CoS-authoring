# Who They Were

Yan was a long-time member of [[Luvash]]'s Vistani family at the [[Vistani Camp]] outside [[Town of Vallaki|Vallaki]].

# What the Party Knows

- Yan was found dead—decapitated—near the [[Luna River]], at the site where [[Arabelle]] was abducted
- He gave wine to [[Alexei]] (Arabelle's cousin) that caused Alexei to fall into a deep sleep
- When Alexei awoke, both Arabelle and Yan were gone
- [[Savid]], a dusk elf guard, discovered Yan's headless body
- The crime scene showed signs of a violent struggle
- A mysterious platinum ring bearing [[Khazan]]'s sigil was found near Yan's body, suggesting it was dropped during the fight
