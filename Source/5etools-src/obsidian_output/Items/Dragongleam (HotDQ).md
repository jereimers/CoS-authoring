# Dragongleam

*Item, Unknown (Magic)*

This spear is enchanted with 10 charges of a [[daylight]] spell for use in twilight or dark forest underbrush. The command phrase is "Tiamat's eyes shine," written in Draconic runes on the spear's crossguard.
