# Manifold Tool

*Wondrous Item, Common* *(requires attunement)*

This tool takes the form of a wrench, a screwdriver, or another basic tool. As a [[Magic|XPHB]] action, you can touch the item and transform it into a type of [[Artisan's Tools|XPHB]] of your choice. Whatever form the tool takes, you have proficiency with it when you use it.
