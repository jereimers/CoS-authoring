## Traits

**Ephemeral.** The wisp can't wear or carry anything.

**Illumination.** The wisp sheds [[Bright Light|XPHB]] in a 20-foot radius and [[Dim Light|XPHB]] for an additional 20 feet.

**Incorporeal Movement.** The wisp can move through other creatures and objects as if they were [[Difficult Terrain|XPHB]]. It takes 5 (**1d10**) Force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.


## Actions

**Shock.** m +4, reach 5 ft. @h11 (**2d8 + 2**) Lightning damage.


## Bonus Actions

**Consume Life.** con DC 10, one living creature the wisp can see within 5 feet that has 0 [[Hit Points|XPHB]]. @actSaveFail The target dies, and the wisp regains 10 (`3d6`) [[Hit Points|XPHB]].

**Vanish.** The wisp and its light have the [[Invisible|XPHB]] condition until the wisp's [[Concentration]] ends on this effect, which ends early immediately after the wisp makes an attack roll or uses Consume Life.
