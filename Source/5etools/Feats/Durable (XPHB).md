# Durable

**Prerequisites:** Level 4

You gain the following benefits.

## Defy Death

You have [[Advantage|XPHB]] on [[Death Saving Throw|XPHB]].

## Speedy Recovery

As a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]], you can expend one of your [[Hit Point Dice|XPHB]], roll the die, and regain a number of [[Hit Points|XPHB]] equal to the roll.
