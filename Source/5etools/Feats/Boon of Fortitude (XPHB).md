# Boon of Fortitude

**Prerequisites:** Level 19

You gain the following benefits.

## Fortified Health

Your [[Hit Points|XPHB]] maximum increases by 40. In addition, whenever you regain [[Hit Points|XPHB]], you can regain additional [[Hit Points|XPHB]] equal to your Constitution modifier. Once you've regained these additional [[Hit Points|XPHB]], you can't do so again until the start of your next turn.
