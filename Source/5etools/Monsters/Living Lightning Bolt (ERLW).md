**Amorphous.** The living spell can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.

## Actions

**Magical Strike.** Melee Spell Attack +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. @h21 (**5d6 + 4**) lightning damage.

**Spell Mimicry {@recharge 5}.** The living spell unleashes a stroke of lightning in a line 100 feet long and 5 feet wide. Each creature in the line must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 28 (**8d6**) lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
