# Skill Expert

**Prerequisites:** Level 4

You gain the following benefits.

## Skill Proficiency

You gain proficiency in one skill of your choice.

## Expertise

Choose one skill in which you have proficiency but lack [[Expertise|XPHB]]. You gain [[Expertise|XPHB]] with that skill.
