## Traits

**Magic Resistance.** The phaerimm has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The phaerimm makes two attacks, using Stinger or Mindwarp Ray in any combination.

**Stinger.** m +3, reach 5 ft. @h4 (**1d6 + 1**) Piercing damage plus 9 (**2d8**) Poison damage.

**Mindwarp Ray.** r +5, range 120 ft. @h6 (**1d6 + 3**) Psychic damage, and the target has the [[Charmed|XPHB]] condition until the start of the phaerimm's next turn.



## Reactions
