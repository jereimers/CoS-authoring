# Dragonscarred

**Prerequisites:** Level 4

You gain the following benefits.

## Damage Resistance

When you gain this feat, choose Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Poison. You have [[Resistance|XPHB]] to the chosen damage type.

## Fearsome Power

When you deal damage to a creature as part of the Attack or Magic action on your turn, you can use the Dragon's Terror benefit of the Cult of the Dragon Initiate feat as a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]] this turn.
