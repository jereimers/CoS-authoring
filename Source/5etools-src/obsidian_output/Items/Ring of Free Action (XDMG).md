# Ring of Free Action

*Item, Rare* *(requires attunement)*

While you wear this ring, [[Difficult Terrain|XPHB]] doesn't cost you extra movement. In addition, magic can neither reduce any of your Speeds nor cause you to have the [[Paralyzed|XPHB]] or [[Restrained|XPHB]] condition.
