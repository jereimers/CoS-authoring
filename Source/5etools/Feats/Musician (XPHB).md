# Musician

You gain the following benefits.

## Instrument Training

You gain proficiency with three Musical Instruments of your choice.

## Encouraging Song

As you finish a [[Short Rest|XPHB]] or [[Long Rest|XPHB]], you can play a song on a [[Musical Instrument|XPHB]] with which you have proficiency and give [[Heroic Inspiration|XPHB]] to allies who hear the song. The number of allies you can affect in this way equals your [[Proficiency|XPHB]].
