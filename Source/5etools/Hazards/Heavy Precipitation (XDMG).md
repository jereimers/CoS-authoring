# Heavy Precipitation

Everything within an area of heavy rain or heavy snowfall is [[Lightly Obscured|XPHB]], and creatures in the area have [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on all Wisdom ([[Perception|XPHB]]) checks. Heavy rain also extinguishes open flames.
