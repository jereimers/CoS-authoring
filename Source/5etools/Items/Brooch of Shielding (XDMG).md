# Brooch of Shielding

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon* *(requires attunement)*

While wearing this brooch, you have [[Resistance|XPHB]] to Force damage, and you have [[Immunity|XPHB]] to damage from the [[Magic Missile|XPHB]] spell.
