# Color Spray

*1st-level illusion*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Self (15-feet cone)  
**Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of colorful sand)  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

You launch a dazzling array of flashing, colorful light. Each creature in a 15-foot [[Cone [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] originating from you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or have the [[Blinded|XPHB]] condition until the end of your next turn.
