# Alert

You gain the following benefits.

## Initiative Proficiency

When you roll [[Initiative|XPHB]], you can add your [[Proficiency|XPHB]] to the roll.

## Initiative Swap

Immediately after you roll [[Initiative|XPHB]], you can swap your [[Initiative|XPHB]] with the [[Initiative|XPHB]] of one willing ally in the same combat. You can't make this swap if you or the ally has the [[Incapacitated|XPHB]] condition.
