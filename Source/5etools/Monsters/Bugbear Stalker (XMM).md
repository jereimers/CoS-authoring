## Traits

**Abduct.** The bugbear needn't spend extra movement to move a creature it is grappling.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The bugbear makes two Javelin or Morningstar attacks.

**Javelin.** m,r +5, reach 10 ft. or range 30/120 ft. @h13 (**3d6 + 3**) Piercing damage.

**Morningstar.** m +5 (with [[Advantage|XPHB]] if the target is [[Grappled|XPHB]] by the bugbear), reach 10 ft. @h12 (**2d8 + 3**) Piercing damage.


## Bonus Actions

**Quick Grapple.** dex DC 13, one Medium or smaller creature the bugbear can see within 10 feet. @actSaveFail The target has the [[Grappled|XPHB]] condition (escape DC 13).
