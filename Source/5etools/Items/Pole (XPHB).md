# Pole

*Item, None*

A Pole is 10 feet long. You can use it to touch something up to 10 feet away. If you must make a Strength ([[Athletics|XPHB]]) check as part of a High or [[Long Jump|XPHB]], you can use the Pole to vault, giving yourself [[Advantage|XPHB]] on the check.
