# Potion of Healing

*Item, Common*

This potion is a magic item. As a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]], you can drink it or administer it to another creature within 5 feet of yourself. The creature that drinks the magical red fluid in this vial regains `2d4 + 2` [[Hit Points|XPHB]]. The potion's red liquid glimmers when agitated.
