# Kasimir's Spellbook

## Description
The spellbook belonging to [[Kasimir Velikov]], a dusk elf mage who has spent centuries studying arcane magic. This tome contains all of Kasimir's prepared spells plus additional spells he has collected over his long life.

The spellbook is referenced as being detailed in **Chapter 5, area N9a** of the original Curse of Strahd adventure, which describes the dusk elf encampment near Vallaki.

## Properties
- **Type:** Spellbook
- **Rarity:** N/A (unique item)
- **Attunement:** No

## Contents
Kasimir uses the **mage** stat block, which means his spellbook contains at minimum the following wizard spells:

### Cantrips

- Light
- Mage hand
- Prestidigitation

### 1st Level

- Shield

### 2nd Level

### 3rd Level
