# Otherworldly Patron

**Class:** [[Warlock]]  
**Level:** 1

At 1st level, you have struck a bargain with an otherworldly being of your choice, such as the Fiend. Your choice grants you features at 1st level and again at 6th, 10th, and 14th level.
