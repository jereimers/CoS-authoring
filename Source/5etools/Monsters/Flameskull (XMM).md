## Traits

**Illumination.** The flameskull sheds [[Bright Light|XPHB]] in a 15-foot radius and [[Dim Light|XPHB]] for an additional 15 feet.

**Magic Resistance.** The flameskull has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**Undead Restoration.** If the flameskull is destroyed, it regains all its [[Hit Points|XPHB]] in 1 hour unless Holy Water is sprinkled on its remains or the [[Dispel Evil and Good|XPHB]] spell is cast on them.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The flameskull makes two Fire Ray attacks.

**Fire Ray.** m,r +5, reach 5 ft. or range 60 ft. @h13 (**3d6 + 3**) Fire damage.
