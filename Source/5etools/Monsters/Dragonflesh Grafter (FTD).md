## Actions

**Multiattack.** The grafter makes one Claw attack and one Greatclub attack.

**Claw.** Melee Weapon Attack +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. @h7 (**1d8 + 3**) slashing damage plus 5 (**1d10**) poison damage.

**Greatclub.** Melee Weapon Attack +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. @h12 (**2d8 + 3**) bludgeoning damage.

**Acid Retch {@recharge 5}.** The grafter retches forth a spray of acidic bile in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking 14 (**4d6**) acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
