# Orb of Time

*Wondrous Item, Common*

This orb can be used as an Arcane Focus.

While holding the orb, you can take a [[Magic|XPHB]] action to determine whether it is morning, afternoon, evening, or nighttime. This property functions only on the Material Plane.
