# Incapacitated

While you have the Incapacitated condition, you experience the following effects.

### Inactive

You can't take any [[Action|XPHB]], [[Bonus Action|XPHB]], or [[Reaction|XPHB]].

### No Concentration

Your [[Concentration]] is broken.

### Speechless

You can't speak.

### Surprised

If you're Incapacitated when you roll [[Initiative|XPHB]], you have [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on the roll.
