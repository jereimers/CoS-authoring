# Ezmerelda's Studded Leather Armor +1

## Description
This is a suit of **+1 studded leather armor** worn by [[Ezmerelda d'Avenir]], the Vistana vampire hunter and protégé of Rudolph van Richten. The armor provides an Armor Class of 12 + Dexterity modifier + 1 (magical bonus), for a total AC of 17 with Ezmerelda's +4 Dexterity modifier.

The armor is part of the equipment Ezmerelda has acquired during her years of monster hunting, and is well-suited for her agile fighting style that relies on speed and precision.
