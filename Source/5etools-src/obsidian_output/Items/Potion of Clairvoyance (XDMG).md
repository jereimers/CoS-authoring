# Potion of Clairvoyance

*Item, Rare*

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the [[Clairvoyance|XPHB]] spell (no [[Concentration]] required).

An eyeball bobs in this potion's yellowish liquid but vanishes when the potion is opened.
