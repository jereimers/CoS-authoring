# Harper Agent

You gain the following benefits.

## Thieves' Cant

You know Thieves' Cant.

## Instrument Training

You gain proficiency with a [[Musical Instrument|XPHB]] of your choice.

## Distracting Melody

When you take the [[Help|XPHB]] action to assist an ally's attack roll, the enemy you're distracting can be within 30 feet of you, rather than within 5 feet of you, provided the enemy can see or hear you.
