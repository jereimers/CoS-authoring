# Mind Sharpener

*Item, Uncommon* *(requires attunement)*

The item has 4 charges. When you fail a Constitution saving throw to maintain [[Concentration]], you can take a [[Reaction|XPHB]] and expend 1 of the item's charges to succeed instead. The item regains `1d4` expended charges daily at dawn.
