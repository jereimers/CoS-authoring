# Slasher

**Prerequisites:** Level 4

You gain the following benefits.

## Hamstring

Once per turn when you hit a creature with an attack that deals Slashing damage, you can reduce the [[Speed|XPHB]] of that creature by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.

## Enhanced Critical

When you score a [[Critical Hit|XPHB]] that deals Slashing damage to a creature, it has [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on attack rolls until the start of your next turn.
