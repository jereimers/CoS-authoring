# Carrion Crawler Mucus

*Item, None*

A creature subjected to Carrion Crawler Mucus must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition for 1 minute. The creature also has the [[Paralyzed|XPHB]] condition while [[Poisoned|XPHB]] in this way. The creature repeats the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
