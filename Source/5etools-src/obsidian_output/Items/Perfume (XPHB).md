# Perfume

*Item, None*

Perfume comes in a 4-ounce vial. For 1 hour after applying Perfume to yourself, you have [[Advantage|XPHB]] on Charisma ([[Persuasion|XPHB]]) checks made to influence an Indifferent Humanoid within 5 feet of yourself.
