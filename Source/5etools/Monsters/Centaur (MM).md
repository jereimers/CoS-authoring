## Traits

**Charge.** If the centaur moves at least 30 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a pike attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 10 (**3d6**) piercing damage.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The centaur makes two attacks: one with its pike and one with its hooves or two with its longbow.

**Pike.** Melee Weapon Attack +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. @h9 (**1d10 + 4**) piercing damage.

**Hooves.** Melee Weapon Attack +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h11 (**2d6 + 4**) bludgeoning damage.

**Longbow.** Ranged Weapon Attack +4 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. @h6 (**1d8 + 2**) piercing damage.
