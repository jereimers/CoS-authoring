## Traits

**Indomitable (2/Day).** The champion rerolls a failed saving throw.

**Second Wind (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest).** As a bonus action, the champion can regain 20 hit points.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The champion makes three attacks with its greatsword or its shortbow.

**Greatsword.** Melee Weapon Attack +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h12 (**2d6 + 5**) slashing damage, plus 7 (**2d6**) slashing damage if the champion has more than half of its total hit points remaining.

**Shortbow.** Ranged Weapon Attack +6 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. @h5 (**1d6 + 2**) piercing damage, plus 7 (**2d6**) piercing damage if the champion has more than half of its total hit points remaining.
