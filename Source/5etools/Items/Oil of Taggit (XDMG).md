# Oil of Taggit

*Item, None*

A creature subjected to Oil of Taggit must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition for 24 hours. The creature also has the [[Unconscious|XPHB]] condition while [[Poisoned|XPHB]] in this way. It wakes up if it takes damage.
