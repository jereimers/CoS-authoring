# Rahadin's Scimitar

## Description
A deadly **scimitar** wielded by [[Rahadin]], the dusk elf chamberlain of [[Castle Ravenloft]] and Strahd's most trusted servant. In Rahadin's hands, this curved blade has ended thousands of lives over nearly five centuries of loyal service to the von Zarovich family.

Though the scimitar itself appears to be a standard weapon, Rahadin's exceptional skill (+6 Dexterity modifier) and centuries of combat experience make it devastatingly effective.

## Properties
- **Type:** Martial Melee Weapon
- **Damage:** 1d6 + 6 slashing
- **Attack Bonus:** +10 to hit
- **Properties:** Finesse, Light
- **Rarity:** Common (mundane weapon)
- **Attunement:** No

## Combat Statistics
As wielded by Rahadin:
- **Attack:** +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target
- **Hit:** 9 (1d6 + 6) slashing damage
- **Multiattack:** Rahadin attacks three times with his scimitar
