# Grappled

While you have the Grappled condition, you experience the following effects.

### Speed 0

Your [[Speed|XPHB]] is 0 and can't increase.

### Attacks Affected

You have [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] on attack rolls against any target other than the grappler.

### Movable

The grappler can drag or carry you when it moves, but every foot of movement costs it 1 extra foot unless you are Tiny or two or more sizes smaller than it.
