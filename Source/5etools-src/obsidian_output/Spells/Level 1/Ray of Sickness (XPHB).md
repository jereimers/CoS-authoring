# Ray of Sickness

*1st-level necromancy*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 60 feet  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

You shoot a greenish ray at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes **2d8** Poison damage and has the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition until the end of your next turn.

## Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot

The damage increases by `2d8` for each spell slot level above 1.
