# Keen Mind

**Prerequisites:** Level 4, INT 13+

You gain the following benefits.

## Lore Knowledge

Choose one of the following skills: [[Arcana|XPHB]], [[History|XPHB]], [[Investigation|XPHB]], [[Nature|XPHB]], or [[Religion|XPHB]]. If you lack proficiency in the chosen skill, you gain proficiency in it, and if you already have proficiency in it, you gain [[Expertise|XPHB]] in it.

## Quick Study

You can take the [[Study|XPHB]] action as a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]].
