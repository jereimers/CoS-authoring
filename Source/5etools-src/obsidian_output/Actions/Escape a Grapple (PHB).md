# Escape a Grapple

**Time:** 1 action

A [[grappled]] creature can use its action to escape. To do so, it must succeed on a Strength ([[Athletics]]) or Dexterity ([[Acrobatics]]) check contested by your Strength ([[Athletics]]) check.
