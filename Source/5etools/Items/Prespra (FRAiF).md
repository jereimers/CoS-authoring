# Prespra

*Item, None*

A creature subjected to Prespra must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or take 3 (`1d6`) Poison damage and have the [[Poisoned|XPHB]] condition. The creature also has the [[Blinded|XPHB]] condition while [[Poisoned|XPHB]] in this way. The creature repeats the save at the start of each of its turns, taking 3 (`1d6`) Poison damage on a failed save. On a successful save, the effect ends for the creature.
