# Climber's Kit

*Item, None*

A Climber's Kit includes boot tips, gloves, pitons, and a harness. As a [[Utilize|XPHB]] action, you can use the Climber's Kit to anchor yourself; when you do, you can't fall more than 25 feet from the anchor point, and you can't move more than 25 feet from there without undoing the anchor as a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]].
