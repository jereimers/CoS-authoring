# Boon of Speed

**Prerequisites:** Level 19

You gain the following benefits.

## Escape Artist

As a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]], you can take the [[Disengage|XPHB]] action, which also ends the [[Grappled|XPHB]] condition on you.

## Quickness

Your [[Speed|XPHB]] increases by 30 feet.
