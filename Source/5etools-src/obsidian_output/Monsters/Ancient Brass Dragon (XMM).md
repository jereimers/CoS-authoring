## Traits

**Legendary Resistance (4/Day, or 5/Day in Lair).** If the dragon fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The dragon makes three Rend attacks. It can replace one attack with a use of (A) Sleep Breath or (B) Spellcasting to cast [[Scorching Ray|XPHB]] (level 3 version).

**Rend.** m +14, reach 15 ft. @h19 (**2d10 + 8**) Slashing damage plus 7 (**2d6**) Fire damage.

**Fire Breath {@recharge 5}.** dex DC 21, each creature in a 90-foot-long, 5-foot-wide [[Line [Area of Effect]|XPHB]]. @actSaveFail 58 (**13d8**) Fire damage. @actSaveSuccess Half damage.

**Sleep Breath.** con DC 21, each creature in a 90-foot [[Cone [Area of Effect]|XPHB]]. @actSaveFail The target has the [[Incapacitated|XPHB]] condition until the end of its next turn, at which point it repeats the save. 2 The target has the [[Unconscious|XPHB]] condition for 10 minutes. This effect ends for the target if it takes damage or a creature within 5 feet of it takes an action to wake it.




## Legendary Actions

The creature can take 3 legendary actions.

**Blazing Light.** The dragon uses Spellcasting to cast [[Scorching Ray|XPHB]] (level 3 version).

**Pounce.** The dragon moves up to half its [[Speed|XPHB]], and it makes one Rend attack.

**Scorching Sands.** dex DC 20, one creature the dragon can see within 120 feet. @actSaveFail 36 (**8d8**) Fire damage, and the target's [[Speed|XPHB]] is halved until the end of its next turn. @actSaveSuccessOrFail The dragon can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.
