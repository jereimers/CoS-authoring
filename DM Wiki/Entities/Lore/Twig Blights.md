# What They Are

Twig blights are small, malevolent plant creatures animated by dark magic. They resemble gnarled, dead saplings with twisted branches for limbs.

# What the Party Knows

- The party encountered twig blights at the [[Luna River Crossroads]] in [[Session 13 - The Lost and The Found|Session 13]]
- Four twig blights ambushed the party alongside two [[Scarecrows|scarecrows]]
- They appeared as small dead saplings scattered across the crossroads until they suddenly jerked to life
- Twig blights scuttled forward on root-like appendages, attacking with their branch-arms
- The creatures were animated by [[Barovia]]'s corruption and dark magic
- They were dispatched relatively easily by the party's combined efforts

# Appearance

- Blackened and gnarled branches and trunks
- Twig-like limbs that serve as weapons
- Small in stature, resembling dead saplings
- Can remain motionless, blending in with natural deadwood until they attack
