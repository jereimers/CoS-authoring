# Spell-Refueling Ring

*Item, Uncommon* *(requires attunement by a spellcaster)*

While wearing this ring, you can recover one expended spell slot as a [[Bonus Action|XPHB]]. The recovered slot can be of level 3 or lower. Once used, the ring can't be used again until the next dawn.
