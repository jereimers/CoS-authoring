# Overview

Black pools of water stand like dark mirrors in and around the muddy roadway. Giant trees loom on both sides of the road, their branches clawing at the mist.

# What the party knows

The Old Svalich Road runs the full length of the [[Barovia|Valley of Barovia]], from the [[Gates of Barovia]] in the east to the [[Village of Krezk]] in the west. On either side, the [[Svalich Woods]] loom darkly.
# Notable places
