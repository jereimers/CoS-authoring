## Traits

**Death Burst.** When the fume drake dies, it explodes in a cloud of noxious fumes. Each creature within 5 feet of the fume drake must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or take 4 (**1d8**) poison damage.

**Unusual Nature.** The fume drake doesn't require food, drink, or sleep.


## Actions

**Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h4 (**1d4 + 2**) fire damage.

**Scalding Breath {@recharge}.** The fume drake exhales a 15-foot cone of scalding steam. Each creature in that area must make a DC 11 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4 (**1d8**) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
