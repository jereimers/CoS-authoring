# Dodge

**Time:** 1 action

lf you take the Dodge action, you gain the following benefits: until the start of your next turn, any attack roll made against you has [[Disadvantage|XPHB]] if you can see the attacker, and you make Dexterity [[Saving Throw|XPHB]] with [[Advantage|XPHB]].

You lose these benefits if you have the [[Incapacitated|XPHB]] condition or if your [[Speed|XPHB]] is 0.
