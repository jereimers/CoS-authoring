## Traits

**Legendary Resistance (3/Day).** If the ki-rin fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

**Magic Resistance.** The ki-rin has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The ki-rin makes two Hoof attacks and one Horn attack, or it makes two Sacred Fire attacks.

**Hoof.** Melee Weapon Attack +9 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. @h10 (**2d4 + 5**) force damage.

**Horn.** Melee Weapon Attack +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. @h14 (**2d8 + 5**) radiant damage.

**Sacred Fire.** Ranged Spell Attack +9 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. @h18 (**3d8 + 5**) radiant damage.




## Legendary Actions

The creature can take 3 legendary actions.

**Move.** The ki-rin moves up to half its speed without provoking [[opportunity attack|]].

**Smite.** The ki-rin makes one Hoof, Horn, or Sacred Fire attack.
