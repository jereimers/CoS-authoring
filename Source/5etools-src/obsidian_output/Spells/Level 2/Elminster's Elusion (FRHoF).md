# Elminster's Elusion

*2nd-level abjuration*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 bonus  
**Range:** Self  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

---

Arcane wards protect you against magic for the duration. You have [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and magical effects. Additionally, if you succeed on a saving throw against a spell or magical effect and would normally take half as much damage, you instead take no damage.
