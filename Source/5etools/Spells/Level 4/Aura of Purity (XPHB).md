# Aura of Purity

*4th-level abjuration*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** {'type': 'emanation', 'distance': {'type': 'feet', 'amount': 30}}  
**Components:** V  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

---

An aura radiates from you in a 30-foot [[Emanation [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] for the duration. While in the aura, you and your allies have [[Resistance|XPHB]] to Poison damage and [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws to avoid or end effects that include the [[Blinded|XPHB]], [[Charmed|XPHB]], [[Deafened|XPHB]], [[Frightened|XPHB]], [[Paralyzed|XPHB]], [[Poisoned|XPHB]], or [[Stunned|XPHB]] condition.
