## Actions

**Multiattack.** The cultist makes three attacks, using Elemental Flail or Elemental Claw in any combination.

**Elemental Flail.** m +7, reach 5 ft. @h25 (**6d6 + 4**) damage of a type chosen by the cultist: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder.

**Elemental Claw.** r +7, range 120 ft. @h22 (**4d10**) damage of a type chosen by the cultist: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, the cultist moves the target up to 10 feet straight toward or away from itself.



## Reactions

**Elemental Absorption (1/Day).** @actTrigger The cultist takes Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder damage. @actResponse The cultist gives itself [[Resistance|XPHB]] to that instance of damage and gains 10 [[Temporary Hit Points|XPHB]].
