## Traits

**Divine Awareness.** The solar knows if it hears a lie.

**Exalted Restoration.** If the solar dies outside Mount Celestia, its body disappears, and it gains a new body instantly, reviving with all its [[Hit Points|XPHB]] somewhere in Mount Celestia.

**Legendary Resistance (4/Day).** If the solar fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

**Magic Resistance.** The solar has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The solar makes two Flying Sword attacks. It can replace one attack with a use of Slaying Bow.

**Flying Sword.** m,r +15, reach 10 ft. or range 120 ft. @h22 (**4d6 + 8**) Slashing damage plus 36 (**8d8**) Radiant damage. @homThe sword magically returns to the solar's hand or hovers within 5 feet of the solar immediately after a ranged attack.

**Slaying Bow.** dex DC 21, one creature the solar can see within 600 feet. @actSaveFail If the creature has 100 [[Hit Points|XPHB]] or fewer, it dies. It otherwise takes 24 (**4d8 + 6**) Piercing damage plus 36 (**8d8**) Radiant damage.




## Legendary Actions

The creature can take 3 legendary actions.

**Blinding Gaze.** con DC 25, one creature the solar can see within 120 feet. @actSaveFail The target has the [[Blinded|XPHB]] condition for 1 minute. @actSaveSuccessOrFail The solar can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.

**Radiant Teleport.** The solar teleports up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see. dex DC 25, each creature in a 10-foot [[Emanation [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] originating from the solar at its destination space. @actSaveFail 11 (**2d10**) Radiant damage. @actSaveSuccess Half damage.
