# Wind Fan

*Wondrous Item, Uncommon*

While holding this fan, you can cast [[Gust of Wind|XPHB]] (save DC 13) from it. Each subsequent time the fan is used before the next dawn, it has a cumulative 20% chance of not working; if the fan fails to work, it tears into useless, nonmagical tatters.
