## Traits

**Aberrant Ground.** The ground in a 10-foot [[Emanation [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] originating from the mouther is [[Difficult Terrain|XPHB]].

**Gibbering.** The mouther babbles incoherently while it doesn't have the [[Incapacitated|XPHB]] condition. wis DC 10, any creature that starts its turn within 20 feet of the mouther while it is babbling. @actSaveFail The target rolls `1d8` to determine what it does during the current turn:

- **1-4:** The target does nothing.
- **5-6:** The target takes no action or [[Bonus Action|XPHB]] and uses all its movement to move in a random direction.
- **7-8:** The target makes a melee attack against a randomly determined creature within its reach or does nothing if it can't make such an attack.


## Actions

**Bite.** m +2, reach 5 ft. @h7 (**2d6**) Piercing damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, it has the [[Prone|XPHB]] condition. The target dies if it is reduced to 0 [[Hit Points|XPHB]] by this attack. Its body is then absorbed into the mouther, leaving only equipment behind.

**Blinding Spittle {@recharge 5}.** dex DC 10, each creature in a 10-foot-radius [[Sphere [Area of Effect]|XPHB]] centered on a point within 30 feet. @actSaveFail 7 (**2d6**) Radiant damage, and the target has the [[Blinded|XPHB]] condition until the end of the mouther's next turn.
