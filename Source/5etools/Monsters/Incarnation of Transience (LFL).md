## Traits

**Ephemeral Movement.** The incarnation can move through other creatures and objects as if they were [[Difficult Terrain|XPHB]], and its movement doesn't provoke Opportunity Attacks. It takes 5 (**1d10**) Force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.

**Magic Resistance.** The incarnation has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


## Actions

**Multiattack.** The incarnation makes three attacks, using Bite or Miasmic Globule in any combination.

**Bite.** m +8, reach 5 ft. @h14 (**2d8 + 5**) Piercing damage.

**Miasmic Globule.** r +8, range 60/120 ft. @h15 (**6d4**) Acid damage, and the target's [[Speed|XPHB]] is reduced by 10 feet until the end of the incarnation's next turn.

**Spectral Stampede.** The incarnation moves up to 80 feet in a straight line. Each creature whose space the incarnation enters is targeted once by the following effect. dex DC 16. @actSaveFail 18 (**4d8**) Force damage. If the target is the incarnation's size or smaller, it has the [[Prone|XPHB]] condition. @actSaveSuccess Half damage only.


## Bonus Actions

**Shape-Shift.** The incarnation changes its size to Medium, Large, or Huge.
