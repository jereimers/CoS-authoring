# What It Is

An ancient shrine consisting of standing stones arranged in a circle, located near the [[Gates of Ravenloft]]. One of the stones bears a carved raven symbol, below which is a three-pointed star. The origin and purpose of the shrine remain unknown.

# What the Party Knows

## From Session 8

The party first encountered the Raven Shrine in [[Session 08 - Hairy Bill, Bairy Hill, and a Coin for the Ladies Three|Session 8]]:

- Located near the Western Gate of [[Village of Barovia]]
- Features several tall standing stones arranged in a rough circle
- One stone is carved with a raven symbol
- The site radiates a sense of quiet, ancient power
- The party left an offering of gold coins at the base of the carved stone
