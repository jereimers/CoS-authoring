# Aid

*2nd-level abjuration*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 30 feet  
**Components:** V, S, M (a strip of white cloth)  
**Duration:** 8 hours

---

Choose up to three creatures within range. Each target's [[Hit Points|XPHB]] maximum and current [[Hit Points|XPHB]] increase by 5 for the duration.

## Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot

Each target's [[Hit Points|XPHB]] increase by 5 for each spell slot level above 2.
