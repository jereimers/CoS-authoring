## Actions

**Multiattack.** The pirate makes three attacks, using Rapier or Pistol in any combination.

**Rapier.** m +7, reach 5 ft. @h13 (**2d8 + 4**) Piercing damage, and the pirate has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on the next attack roll it makes before the end of this turn.

**Pistol.** r +7, range 30/90 ft. @h15 (**2d10 + 4**) Piercing damage.


## Bonus Actions

**Captain's Charm.** wis DC 14, one creature the pirate can see within 30 feet. @actSaveFail The target has the [[Charmed|XPHB]] condition until the start of the pirate's next turn.


## Reactions

**Riposte.** @actTrigger The pirate is hit by a melee attack roll while holding a weapon. @actResponse The pirate adds 3 to its AC against that attack, possibly causing it to miss. On a miss, the pirate makes one Rapier attack against the triggering creature if within range.
