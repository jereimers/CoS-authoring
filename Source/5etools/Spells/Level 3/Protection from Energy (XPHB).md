# Protection from Energy

*3rd-level abjuration*

---

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

---

For the duration, the willing creature you touch has [[Resistance|XPHB]] to one damage type of your choice: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder.
