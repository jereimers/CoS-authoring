# Charmed

While you have the Charmed condition, you experience the following effects.

### Can't Harm the Charmer

You can't attack the charmer or target the charmer with damaging abilities or [[Magical Effect|XPHB]].

### Social Advantage

The charmer has [[Advantage|XPHB]] on any ability check to interact with you socially.
