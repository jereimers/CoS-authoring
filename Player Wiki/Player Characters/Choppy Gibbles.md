**Key Abilities:** A charismatic spellcaster with divine power coursing through their veins. Choppy excels at deception and persuasion, backed by proficiency with a Disguise Kit and Forgery Kit. Their Charlatan background grants them a false identity complete with documentation and established acquaintances.

# Backstory (public)

Choppy comes from a noble family, though the details of their past are shrouded in mystery—as befitting a charlatan. At some point, their lands and title were stolen from them, and they became separated from their family. Now, Choppy travels the world using their wits, charm, and divine gifts to get by, always hoping that one day they might reunite with their lost family and reclaim what was taken from them.

# Personality & Beliefs

**Personality Traits:**
- If someone is in trouble, always ready to lend help
- Uses flattery as a preferred method for getting what they want  
- Has a joke for every occasion—especially when humor is inappropriate

**Ideals:**
- *Respect:* People deserve to be treated with dignity and respect
- *Independence:* A free spirit who won't be told what to do
- *Charity:* Distributes acquired money to those who truly need it

**Bonds:**
- Has a family somewhere out there, hoping to see them again someday
- Seeks to reclaim their noble lands and title from those who stole them

**Flaws:**
- Convinced of the significance of their own destiny, blind to shortcomings and risk of failure
- Will run and preserve their own hide if the going gets truly tough
