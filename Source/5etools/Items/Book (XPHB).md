# Book

*Item, None*

A Book contains fiction or nonfiction. If you consult an accurate nonfiction Book about its topic, you gain a +5 bonus to Intelligence ([[Arcana|XPHB]], [[History|XPHB]], [[Nature|XPHB]], or [[Religion|XPHB]]) checks you make about that topic.
